Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category, and let \[ 0 \rightarrow A^\bdot \rightarrow B^\bdot \rightarrow C^\bdot \rightarrow 0 \] be a short exact sequence of complexes in \(\mathcal{A}\). Then there is an induced long exact sequence \[ \cdots \rightarrow H^i(A) \rightarrow H^i(B) \rightarrow H^i(C) \rightarrow H^{i + 1}(A) \rightarrow \cdots . \]
