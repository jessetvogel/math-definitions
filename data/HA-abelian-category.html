An abelian category is a category such that
  • every \(\Hom(A, B)\) is an abelian group, and composition is bilinear,
  • it has a zero object,
  • it has all binary biproducts,
  • it has all kernels and cokernels,
  • every monomoprhism is the kernel of some morphism, and every epimoprhism is the cokernel of some morphism.
