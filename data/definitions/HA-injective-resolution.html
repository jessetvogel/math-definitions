Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category and \(M\) an object in \(\mathcal{A}\). An injective resolution of \(M\) is an exact sequence \[ 0 \to M \to I^0 \to I^1 \to \cdots \] where all \(I^i\) are injective objects. Equivalently, this can be seen as a quasi-isomorphism \(M[0] \to I^\bdot\) in \(\text{Comp}(\mathcal{A})\).
