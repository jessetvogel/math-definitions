Let \(X\) be a topological space, let \(\pi : P \to X\) be a principal \(G\)-bundle, and let \(\rho : G \to \textup{Aut}(F)\) be a continuous right action of \(G\) on a space \(F\). There is a left action of \(G\) on \(P \times F\) given by \(g \cdot (p, f) = (g \cdot p, f \cdot \rho(g^{-1}))\), and the quotient \((P \times F) / G\) is called the associated bundle to \(P\) of \(\rho\).
