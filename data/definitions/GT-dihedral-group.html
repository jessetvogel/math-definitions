The dihedral group \(D_n\) is the group of symmetries of the regular \(n\)-gon in the plane. Its order is \(2n\), and it has a presentation \[ D_n = \langle \rho, \sigma \mid \rho^n = 1, \sigma^2 = 1, \sigma \rho \sigma^{-1} = \rho^{-1} \rangle , \] where \(\rho\) corresponds to a rotation of \(2 \pi / n\) and \(\sigma\) corresponds to a reflection.
