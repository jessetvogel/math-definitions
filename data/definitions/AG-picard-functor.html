Let \(f : X \to S\) be a scheme over \(S\). The absolute Picard functor is the functor that assigns to any \(T \to S\) the Picard group \(\text{Pic}(X \times_S T)\), \[ \textup{Pic}_X : \textbf{Sch}/S \to \textbf{Ab}, \quad T \mapsto \textup{Pic}(X \times_S T) . \] The relative Picard functor is the functor \[ \textup{Pic}_{X/S} : \textbf{Sch}/S \to \textbf{Ab}, \quad T \mapsto \textup{Pic}(X \times_S T) / f_T^* \textup{Pic}(T) . \]
