A bialgebra over a field \(k\) is an algebra \(A\) over \(k\) together with morphisms of \(k\)-algebras \(\Delta : A \to A \otimes A\) (the comultiplication) and \(\varepsilon : A \to k\) (the counit), satisfying
  • (coassociativity) \((\Delta \otimes \id) \circ \Delta = (\id \otimes \Delta) \circ \Delta\),
  • (counit) \((\varepsilon \otimes \id) \circ \Delta = \id = (\id \otimes \varepsilon) \otimes \Delta\).
