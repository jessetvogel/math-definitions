Let \(\Omega\) be a set and \(\mathcal{P}(\Omega)\) its power set. An outer measure on \(\Omega\) is a mapping \(\mu^* : \mathcal{P}(\Omega) \to [0, \infty]\) such that
  • \(\mu^*(\varnothing) = 0\),
  • \(\mu^*(A) \le \mu^*(B)\) for all \(A \subset B \subset \Omega\),
  • \(\mu^*\left(\cup_{n = 1}^{\infty} A_n \right) \le \sum_{n = 1}^{\infty} \mu^*(A_n)\) for all sequences \((A_n)_{n \in \NN}\) of subsets of \(\Omega\).
