The braid group on \(n\) strands is the group \[ B_n = \left\langle \sigma_1, \ldots, \sigma_{n - 1} \;\bigg|\; \begin{array}{cc} \sigma_i \sigma_{i + 1} \sigma_i = \sigma_{i + 1} \sigma_i \sigma_{i + 1} \text{ for } 1 \le i \le n - 2 \\ \sigma_i \sigma_j = \sigma_j \sigma_i \text{ for } |i - j| > 1 \end{array} \right\rangle . \] Intuitively, its elements are ways to ‘braid‘ \(n\) strands, where \(\sigma_i\) interchanges strands \(i\) and \(i + 1\).
