Let \(M\) be a smooth manifold and \(p : E \to M\) a vector bundle on \(M\) with a connection \(\nabla\). Then the horizontal distribution on \(E\) is the distribution \(\mathcal{H} \subset TE\) given by \[ \mathcal{H}_x = \left\{ \frac{d}{dt}\Big|_{t = 0} s(t) : s : [-1, 1] \to E \text{ with } s(0) = x \text{ is a parallel section over } \gamma = p \circ s \right\} \] for all points \(x \in E\).
That is, a section \(s : M \to E\) is parallel if and only if it is an integral submanifold of \(\mathcal{H}\).
