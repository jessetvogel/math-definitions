Let \(M\) be a smooth manifold. An almost Diract structure on \(M\) is a subbundle \(L \subset TM \oplus T^*M\) which is maximal isotropic with respect to symmetric bilinear form \(\langle X + \xi, Y + \eta \rangle = \xi(Y) + \eta(X)\). If moreover \(L\) is closed under the Courant bracket, it is called integrable, or simply a Dirac structure.
