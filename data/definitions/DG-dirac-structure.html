Let \(M\) be a smooth manifold. An almost Dirac structure on \(M\) is a subbundle \(L \subset TM \oplus T^*M\) of the generalized tangent bundle of \(M\), such that \(L\) maximal isotropic with respect to symmetric bilinear form \(\langle X + \xi, Y + \eta \rangle = \xi(Y) + \eta(X)\). If moreover \(L\) is closed under the Courant bracket, it is called integrable, or simply a Dirac structure.
