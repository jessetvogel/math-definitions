Let \(f \in R[X]\) be a polynomial over some domain \(R\). Then \(f\) can be written as \[ f = a \prod_{i = 1}^n (X - \alpha_i) \] in some sufficiently large extension \(R' \supset R\) of domains, with \(\alpha_i \in R'\) and \(a \in R\). The discriminant of \(f\) is defined as \[ \Delta(f) = a^{2n - 2} \prod_{1 \le i < j \le n} (\alpha_i - \alpha_j)^2 \in R . \]
