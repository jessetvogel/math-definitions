Let \(R\) be a commutative ring, \(I \subset R\) an ideal and \(M\) a finitely generated \(R\)-module such that \(IM \subsetneq M\). Then the \(I\)-depth of \(M\) is defined as \[ \text{depth}_I(M) = \min \{ i \in \ZZ : \text{Ext}^i(R/I, M) \ne 0 \} . \] When \(R\) is a local ring, one usually takes \(I\) equal to be the maximal ideal \(\mathfrak{m}\).
