Let \(X\) be a topological space. A stratification of \(X\) is a family \(\{ X_i \}_{i \in I}\) of locally closed subsets of \(X\) and a partial order on \(I\) such that
  • \(X = \bigcup_{i \in I} X_i\),
  • \(X_i \cap X_j = \varnothing\) whenever \(i \ne j\),
  • \(\overline{X}_i \subset \bigcup_{j \le i} X_j\).
