Let \(X\) be a variety over a field \(k\). A resolution \(\pi : \widetilde{X} \to X\) is crepant if the canonical isomorphism \(\pi^* K_U \cong K_{\pi^{-1}(U)}\) extends to an isomorphism \(\pi^* K_X \cong K_{\widetilde{X}}\), where \(U \subset X\) is a non-singular open subset such that \(\pi : \pi^{-1}(U) \to U\) is an isomorphism.
