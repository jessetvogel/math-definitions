Let \(R\) be a commutative ring, and \(S \subset R\) a multiplicative set (that is, \(1 \in S\) and \(xy \in S\) for all \(x, y \in S\)). Then the localization of \(R\) w.r.t. \(S\) is the ring \[ S^{-1} R = R \times S / \sim{} \quad \text{ where } (r_1, s_1) \sim{} (r_2, s_2) \text{ if } t(r_1 s_2 - r_2 s_1) = 0 \text{ for some } t \in S . \]
