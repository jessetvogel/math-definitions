Let \((M, \omega)\) be a symplectic manifold and \(G\) a Lie group with a symplectic action on \(M\). A smooth map \(\mu : M \to \mathfrak{g}^*\) is a weak moment map for the action if \[ d \langle \mu, \xi \rangle = \omega(X_\xi, -) \] for all \(\xi \in \mathfrak{g}\), and a moment map if moreover \(\mu\) is \(G\)-equivariant with respect to the co-adjoint representation of \(G\), that is, \[ \mu(g \cdot p) = \textup{Ad}_G^* (g) \mu(p) \] for all \(p \in M\) and \(g \in G\).
