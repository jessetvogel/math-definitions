Let \(F : \mathcal{A} \to \mathcal{B}\) be an exact functor between abelian categories, and suppose \(\mathcal{A}\) has enough injectives. For any cochain complex \(A^\bdot\) in \(\mathcal{A}\), the hypercohomology of \(A^\bdot\), denoted \[ \mathbb{H}^i(A^\bdot) \] is given by the cohomology \(H^i(F(I^\bdot))\) for any injective resolution \(I^\bdot\) of \(A^\bdot\), that is, \(I^\bdot\) is a complex of injective objects in \(\mathcal{A}\) which is quasi-isomorphic to \(A^\bdot\).
