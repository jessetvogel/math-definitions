Given a partition \(\lambda = (\lambda_1, \ldots, \lambda_k)\) of a non-negative integer \(n\), the conjugate partition \(\lambda' = (\lambda'_1, \ldots, \lambda'_\ell)\) to \(\lambda\) is given by \[ \lambda'_i = | \{ 1 \le j \le k \mid \lambda_j \ge i \} | \] for \(1 \le i \le \ell\), where \(\ell = \lambda_1\).
