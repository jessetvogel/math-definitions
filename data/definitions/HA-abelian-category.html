An abelian category is an additive category category \(\mathcal{A}\) in which
  • every morphism \(f\) has a kernel (that is, an equalizer of \(f\) and \(0\)) and a cokernel (that is, a coequalizer of \(f\) and \(0\)),
  • every monomorphism is the kernel of some morphism, and every epimorphism is the cokernel of some morphism.
