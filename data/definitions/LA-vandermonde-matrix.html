A Vandermonde matrix is an \(m \times n\) matrix of the form \[ \begin{pmatrix} 1 & x_1 & x_1^2 & \cdots & x_1^{n - 1} \\ 1 & x_2 & x_2^2 & \cdots & x_2^{n - 1} \\ 1 & x_3 & x_3^2 & \cdots & x_3^{n - 1} \\ \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ 1 & x_m & x_m^2 & \cdots & x_m^{n - 1} \end{pmatrix} \] for some \(x_1, \ldots, x_m\).
