For \(p\) an odd prime number and \(d\) an integer, the Legendre symbol \(\left(\tfrac{d}{p}\right)\) is defined as \[ \left(\frac{d}{p}\right) = \left\{ \begin{array}{cl} 0 & \text{ if $a \equiv 0 \text{ mod } p$} , \\ 1 & \text{ if $a \text{ mod } p$ is a square in $\ZZ/p\ZZ$} , \\ -1 & \text{ otherwise} . \end{array} \right. \]
