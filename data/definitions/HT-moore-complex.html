Let \(A_\bdot\) be a simplicial abelian group. The Moore complex of \(A_\bdot\) is the chain complex \(C(A_\bdot)\) given by \[ C(A_\bdot)_n = A_n \quad \textup{ with differential } \quad \partial_n = \sum_{i = 0}^{n} (-1)^i d_{n, i} , \] where \(d_{n, i} : A_n \to A_{n - 1}\) are the face maps of \(A_\bdot\). The normalized Moore complex of \(A_\bdot\) is the chain complex \(N(A_\bdot)\) given by \[ N(A_\bdot)_n = \bigcap_{i = 0}^{n - 1} \ker d_{n, i} \quad \text{ with differential } \quad \partial_n = d_{n, n} . \]
