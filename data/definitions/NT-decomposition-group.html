Let \(K\) be a field with valuation \(\phi\), and let \(L/K\) be a finite Galois extension with valuation \(\psi\) extending \(\phi\). The decomposition group of \(\psi\) in \(L/K\) is \[ G_\psi = \{ \sigma \in \operatorname{Gal}(L/K) \mid \psi(\sigma(x)) = \psi(x) \textup{ for all } x \in L \} . \] The decomposition field of \(\psi\) in \(L/K\) is the corresponding invariant field \(L^{G_\psi}\).
