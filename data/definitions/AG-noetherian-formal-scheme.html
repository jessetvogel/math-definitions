A noetherian formal scheme is a locally ringed space \((\mathfrak{X}, \mathcal{O}_\mathfrak{X})\), which has a finite open cover \(\{ \mathfrak{U}_i \}\) such that for each \(i\), the pair \((\mathfrak{U}_i, \mathcal{O}_\mathfrak{X}|_{\mathfrak{U}_i})\) is isomorphic to the formal completion of some noetherian scheme \(X\) along a closed subscheme \(Y \subset X\).
