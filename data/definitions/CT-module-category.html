Let \((\mathcal{C}, \otimes, \textbf{1})\) be a monoidal category. A (left) module category over \(\mathcal{C}\) is a category \(\mathcal{M}\) together with a functor \(\boxtimes : \mathcal{C} \times \mathcal{M} \to \mathcal{M}\) and natural isomorphisms \[ \begin{aligned} \textup{(associator)}& \quad \alpha : - \boxtimes (- \boxtimes -) \Rightarrow (- \otimes -) \boxtimes - \\ \textup{(unitor)}& \quad \lambda : \textbf{1} \boxtimes - \Rightarrow \id_\mathcal{M} \end{aligned} \] which are compatible with the associator and left and right unitor of \(\mathcal{C}\).
