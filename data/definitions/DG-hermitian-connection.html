Let \(M\) be a smooth manifold and let \(E \to M\) be a Hermitian vector bundle. A Hermitian connection on \(E\) is a connection \(\nabla\) on \(E\) such that \[ X \langle s, t \rangle = \langle \nabla_X s, t \rangle + \langle s, \nabla_X t \rangle \] for all vector fields \(X\) and all \(s, t \in \Gamma(E)\).
