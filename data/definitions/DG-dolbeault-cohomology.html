Let \(M\) be a complex smooth manifold, and consider the Dolbeault operator \[ \overline{\partial} : \Omega^{p, q}(M) \to \Omega^{p, q + 1}(M) . \] Since \(\overline{\partial}^2 = 0\), one can form the complex \((\Omega^{p, \bdot}(M), \overline{\partial})\), and the corresponding cohomology group \(H^{p, q}(M, \CC)\) is called the \((p, q)\)-th Dolbeault cohomology group.
