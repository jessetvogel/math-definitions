Let \((V, \Phi)\) be a root system. A closed subsystem of \((V, \Phi)\) is a subset \(\Phi' \subset \Phi\) such that
  • (subsystem) if \(\alpha, \beta \in \Phi'\), then \(\beta - \frac{2 \langle \alpha, \beta \rangle}{\langle \alpha, \alpha \rangle} \alpha \in \Phi'\),
  • (closed) if \(\alpha, \beta \in \Phi'\) and \(\alpha + \beta \in \Phi\), then \(\alpha + \beta \in \Phi'\),
If \(\Phi'\) only satisfies the second condition, it is simply called a closed subset of \((V, \Phi)\).
