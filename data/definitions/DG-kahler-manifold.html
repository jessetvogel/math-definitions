A KÃ¤hler manifold is a smooth manifold \(M\) with three compatible structures: a complex structure \(J\), a Riemannian structure \(g\), and a symplectic structure \(\omega\). Compatibility means that \[ g(X, Y) = \omega(X, JY) \] for all vector fields \(X, Y\) on \(M\).
Note that two-out-of-three structures determines the other structure.
