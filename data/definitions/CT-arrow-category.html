Given a category \(\mathcal{C}\), the arrow category \(\mathcal{C}^\rightarrow\) is the category whose
  • objects are morphisms \(f : X \to Y\) of \(\mathcal{C}\),
  • morphisms from \(f : X \to Y\) to \(f' : X' \to Y'\) are pairs \((g, h)\), where \(g : X \to X'\) and \(h : Y \to Y'\) such that \(f' \circ g = h \circ f\).
