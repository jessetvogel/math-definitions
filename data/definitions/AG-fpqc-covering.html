An fpqc covering of a scheme \(X\) is a family of morphisms \(\left\{ f_i : X_i \to X \right\}_{i \in I}\) that are all flat, and such that for every affine open \(U \subset X\) there exists a finite subset \(J \subset I\) and affine opens \(V_j \subset X_j\) for each \(j \in J\) such that \(U = \bigcup_{j \in J} f_j(V_j)\). That is, every affine open in \(X\) can be covered by finitely many affine opens from the \(X_i\).
