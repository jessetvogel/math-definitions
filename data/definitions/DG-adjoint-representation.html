The adjoint representation of a Lie group \(G\) is a representation onto its Lie algebra \(\mathfrak{g} = T_e G\) given by \[ \text{Ad}_G : G \to \text{GL}(\mathfrak{g}), \quad g \mapsto \text{Ad}_g := d c_g(e) , \] where \(c_g : G \to G, h \mapsto g h g^{-1}\) denotes the conjugation map.
The co-adjoint representation \(\text{Ad}_G^* : G \to \text{GL}(\mathfrak{g}^*)\) is the dual representation of \(\text{Ad}_G\).
