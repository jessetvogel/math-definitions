Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category and \(M\) an object in \(\mathcal{A}\). A projective resolution of \(M\) is an exact sequence \[ \cdots \to P^1 \to P^0 \to M \to 0 \] where all \(P^i\) are projective objects. Equivalently, this can be seen as a quasi-isomorphism \(M[0] \to P^\bdot\) in \(\textup{Comp}(\mathcal{A})\).
