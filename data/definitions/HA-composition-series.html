A composition series of an object \(A\) in an abelian category \(\mathcal{A}\) is a sequence of subobjects \[ 0 = A_0 \subsetneq A_1 \subsetneq \cdots \subsetneq A_n = A \] such that the quotients \(A_i/A_{i - 1}\), known as the composition factors, are simple for all \(1 \le i \le n\). If \(A\) has a composition series, the integer \(n\) only depends on \(A\) and is called the length of \(A\).
