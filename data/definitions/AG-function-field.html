Let \(X\) be an irreducible variety over a field \(k\). The function field of \(X\) is the field \(K(X)\) over \(k\) given by \[ K(X) = \{ (U, f) : U \subset X \textup{ dense open and } f \in \mathcal{O}_X(U) \} / \sim{} \] where \((U, f) \sim{} (V, g)\) if and only if \(f|_{U \cap V} = g|_{U \cap V}\).
