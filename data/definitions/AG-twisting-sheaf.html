Let \(S\) be a graded ring and let \(X = \Proj S\). For any \(n \in \ZZ\), the twisting sheaf \(\mathcal{O}_X(n)\) is the sheaf associated to \(S(n)\), where \(S(n)_d = S_{d + n}\).
For any sheaf of \(\mathcal{O}_X\)-modules \(\mathcal{F}\), the twisted sheaf \(\mathcal{F}(n)\) is given by \(\mathcal{F} \otimes_{\mathcal{O}_X} \mathcal{O}_X(n)\).
Note that sheaves \(\mathcal{O}_X(n)\) are all invertible sheaves, and that \(\mathcal{O}_X(n) \otimes \mathcal{O}_X(m) \simeq \mathcal{O}_X(n + m)\).
