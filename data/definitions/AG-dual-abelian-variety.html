Let \(X\) be an abelian variety over a field \(k\). The dual abelian variety \(X^t\) of \(X\) is the variety over \(k\) representing the functor \[ T \mapsto \operatorname{Pic}_{X/k}(T)^0 , \] where \(\operatorname{Pic}_{X/k}(T)\) is the relative Picard functor. There is a group law on \(X^t\), given by the tensor product of line bundles, making \(X^t\) into an abelian variety over \(k\).
