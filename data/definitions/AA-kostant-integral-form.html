Let \(\mathfrak{g}\) be a semisimple Lie algebra over \(\CC\), with Chevalley basis \(\{ H_1, \ldots, H_\ell \} \cup \{ X_\alpha \;:\; \alpha \in \Phi \}\). The Kostant \(\ZZ\)-form of \(\mathfrak{g}\) is the \(\ZZ\)-subalgebra \(U_\ZZ(\mathfrak{g})\) of the universal enveloping algebra \(U(\mathfrak{g})\) generated by the elements \(X_\alpha^r / r!\) for all \(\alpha \in \Phi\) and \(r \ge 1\).
