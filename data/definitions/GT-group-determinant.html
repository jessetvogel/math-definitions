Let \(G = \{ g_1, \ldots, g_n \}\) be a finite group. The group determinant of \(G\) is the determinant of the matrix \[ \begin{pmatrix} x_{g_1 g_1^{-1}} & x_{g_1 g_2^{-1}} & \cdots & x_{g_1 g_n^{-1}} \\ x_{g_2 g_1^{-1}} & x_{g_2 g_2^{-1}} & \cdots & x_{g_2 g_n^{-1}} \\ \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ x_{g_n g_1^{-1}} & x_{g_n g_2^{-1}} & \cdots & x_{g_n g_n^{-1}} \end{pmatrix} \] as a polynomial in \(\ZZ[x_{g_1}, \ldots, x_{g_n}]\).
