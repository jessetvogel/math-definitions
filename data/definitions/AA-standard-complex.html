Let \(k\) be a field and \(A\) an \(k\)-algebra. The standard complex is the chain complex with \(A^{\otimes n + 2}\) at degree \(n\), \[ \cdots \xrightarrow{d_{n + 1}} A^{\otimes n + 2} \xrightarrow{d_n} \cdots \xrightarrow{d_1} A^{\otimes 2} \xrightarrow{d_0} A \to 0 \] with differential given by \[ d_n(a_0 \otimes \cdots \otimes a_{n + 1}) = \sum_{i = 0}^{n} (-1)^i a_0 \otimes \cdots \otimes a_i a_{i + 1} \otimes \cdots \otimes a_n . \]
