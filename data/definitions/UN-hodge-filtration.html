A Hodge filtration is an equivalent description of a pure Hodge structure of weight \(k \in \ZZ\), given by a filtration \[ H_\CC \supset \cdots \supset F^p \supset F^{p + 1} \supset \cdots \quad \text{ with } \quad F^p \oplus \overline{F^q} = H_\CC \text{ for } p + q = k + 1 . \] A Hodge filtration can be obtained from a pure Hodge structure, and vice versa, via \[ F^p = \bigoplus_{r \ge p} H^{r, k - r} \quad \text{and} \quad H^{p, q} = F^p \cap \overline{F^q} . \]
