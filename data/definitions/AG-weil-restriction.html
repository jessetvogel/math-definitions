Let \(f : S' \to S\) be a morphism of schemes. Given a scheme \(X\) over \(S'\), if the functor \[ \textup{Res}_{S'/S}(X) : \textbf{Sch}_S^\textup{op} \to \textbf{Set}, \quad T \mapsto \Hom_{S'}(T \times_S S', X) \] is representable, then the corresponding scheme \(\textup{Res}_{S'/S}(X)\) over \(S\) is called the Weil restriction of \(X\) along \(f\).
