Let \(G\) be a group, \(H \subset G\) a subgroup, and \(\rho : H \to \textup{GL}(V)\) a representation of \(H\) over a field \(k\). The induced representation of \(V\) is the natural representation of \(G\) on \[ \textup{Ind}_H^G (\rho) = k[G] \otimes_{k[H]} V , \] where \(k[G]\) denotes the group ring of \(G\). Indeed, \(V\), being a representation of \(H\), is a \(k[H]\)-module, and \(k[G]\) is naturally a \(k[G]\)-\(k[H]\)-bimodule.
