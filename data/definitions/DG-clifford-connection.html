Let \((M, g)\) be a Riemannian manifold and let \(c\) be a Clifford action on a complex vector bundle \(E \to M\). A Clifford connection on \(E\) is a Hermitian connection on \(E\) such that \[ \nabla_X c(Y) s = c(Y) \nabla_X s + c(\nabla^{\textup{LC}}_X Y) s \] for all vector fields \(X\) and \(Y\) and all \(s \in \Gamma(E)\), where \(\nabla^{\textup{LC}}\) denotes the Levi-Civita connection on \(M\).
