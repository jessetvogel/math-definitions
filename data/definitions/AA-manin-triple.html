A Manin triple \((\mathfrak{g}, \mathfrak{g}^+, \mathfrak{g}^-)\) consists of a Lie algebra \(\mathfrak{g}\) with a non-degenerate invariant symmetric bilinear form \(\langle \cdot, \cdot \rangle\) on \(\mathfrak{g}\), together with two isotropic complementary subspaces \(\mathfrak{g}^+, \mathfrak{g}^- \subset \mathfrak{g}\).
