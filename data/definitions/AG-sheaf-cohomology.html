Let \(X\) be a topological space. The global section functor from sheaves on \(X\) to abelian groups, \[ \Gamma(X, -) : \textup{Sh}(X) \to \textbf{Ab} , \quad \mathcal{F} \mapsto \Gamma(X, \mathcal{F}) \] is left exact, and since \(\textup{Sh}(X)\) has enough injectives, one can form the right derived functors \(H^i(X, -) = \textup{R}^i \Gamma(X, -)\). The group \[ H^i(X, \mathcal{F}) \] is called the \(i\)-th cohomology group of \(X\) with coefficients in \(\mathcal{F}\).
