Let \(\chi_\lambda\) be a complex character of the symmetric group \(S_n\) corresponding to a partition \(\lambda = (\lambda_1, \ldots, \lambda_k)\) of \(n \ge 1\). Let \(C_i\) be the conjugacy class of permutations of cycle type \(i = (i_1, i_2, \ldots, i_n)\), that is, those permutations that have \(i_1\) \(1\)-cycles, \(i_2\) \(2\)-cycles, ..., and \(i_n\) \(n\)-cycles. The Frobenius formula states that \(\chi_\lambda(C_i)\) equals the coefficient of \(x_1^{\ell_1} \cdots x_k^{\ell_k}\) in \[ \prod_{1 \le i < j \le k} (x_i - x_j) \prod_{j = 1}^{n} (x_1^j + \cdots + x_k^j)^{i_j} , \] where \(\ell_j = \lambda_j + k - j\).
