A Kan fibration is a map of simplicial sets \(f : X \to Y\) such that for any \(1 \le i \le n\) and for any maps \(s : \Lambda^n_i \to X\) and \(y : \Delta^n \to Y\) with \(f \circ s = y \circ i\) (where \(i : \Lambda^n_i \to \Delta^n\) is the inclusion) there exists a map \(x : \Delta^n \to X\) such that \(s = x \circ i\).
