A Cartier divisor on a scheme \(X\) is a global section of \(\mathcal{M}^*/\mathcal{O}_X^*\), where \(\mathcal{M}\) denotes the sheaf of total quotient rings of \(\mathcal{O}_X\), which is the sheaf associated to the presheaf that assigns to each open \(U \subset X\) the total quotient ring of \(\mathcal{O}_X(U)\).
