Let \(R\) be an order in a number field \(K\), admitting \(r\) real embeddings and \(2s\) complex embeddings, and write \(\mu_R\) for the group of roots of unity in \(R\). Then the Dirichlet unit theorem states that \(\mu_R\) is finite, and \(R^*/\mu_R\) is a free abelian group of rank \(r + s - 1\).
In particular, \[ R^* = \mu_R \times \langle \eta_1 \rangle \times \langle \eta_2 \rangle \cdots \langle \eta_{r + s - 1} \rangle , \] for some fundamental units \(\eta_i \in R^*\).
