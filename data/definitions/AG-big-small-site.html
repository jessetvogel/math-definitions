Let \(X\) be a scheme, and let \(\tau\) be any of the properties in \(\{\) Zariski, Ã©tale, smooth, fppf, fpqc \(\}\).
The big \(\tau\)-site of \(X\) is the site \((\textbf{Sch}/X)_\tau\) of schemes over \(X\) with the \(\tau\)-topology.
The small \(\tau\)-site of \(X\) is the full subcategory \(X_\tau\) of \((\textbf{Sch}/X)_\tau\) whose objects are schemes \(T\) over \(X\) whose structure morphism \(T \to X\) is part of a \(\tau\)-covering.
