Let \(G\) be a topological group acting on a topological space \(X\), and \(A\) an abelian group. The equivariant cohomology of \(X\) with coefficients in \(A\) is \[ H_G^\bdot(X; A) = H^\bdot(EG \times_G X; A), \] that is, the singular cohomology of \(EG \times_G X = (EG \times X) / G\), where \(EG\) denotes a universal \(G\)-bundle and \(G\) acts diagonally on \(EG \times X\).
