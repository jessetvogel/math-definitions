Let \(G\) be a connected, reductive algebraic group over an algebraically closed field \(k\), let \(B \subset G\) be a Borel subgroup, and let \(W\) be the Weyl group of \(G\) corresponding to a maximal torus \(T\) of \(B\). The Bruhat decomposition of \(G\) is the decomposition \[ G = BWB = \bigsqcup_{w \in W} BwB . \]
