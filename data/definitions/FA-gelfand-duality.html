Gelfand duality states that the category of compact Hausdorff spaces is equivalent to the (opposite) category of commutative C*-algebras. It is given by the functors \[ \textbf{Top}_\textup{cpt} \stackrel{\overset{C}{\longrightarrow}}{\underset{\textup{sp}}{\longleftarrow}} \textbf{C*Alg}_\textup{comm}^\textup{op} , \] where \(C\) assigns to a space \(X\) the C*-algebra of continuous functions \[ C(X) = \{ f : X \to \CC \textup{ with $f$ continuous} \} , \] and \(\textup{sp}\) is given by the Gelfand spectrum.
