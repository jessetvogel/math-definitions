Let \(\mathcal{T}\) be a triangulated category. An object \(X\) of \(\mathcal{T}\) is tilting if
  • \(X\) generates \(\mathcal{T}\) as triangulated category,
  • \(\Hom_\mathcal{T}(X, X[\ell]) = 0\) for \(\ell \ne 0\),
  • the category of \(\Hom_{\mathcal{T}}(X, X)\)-modules has finite global dimension.
