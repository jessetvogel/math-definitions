Let \(X\) be a scheme over a field \(k\), and \(\ell\) a prime number different from the characteristic of \(k\). The \(\ell\)-adic cohomology of \(X\) is given by the inverse limit \[ H^i(X, \ZZ_\ell) = \varprojlim_{n \in \NN} H^i(X, \ZZ/\ell^n \ZZ) \] and \[ H^i(X, \QQ_\ell) = H^i(X, \ZZ_\ell) \otimes_{\ZZ_\ell} \QQ_\ell , \] where \(\ZZ_\ell\) denotes the \(\ell\)-adic integers and \(\QQ_\ell\) its field of fractions.
