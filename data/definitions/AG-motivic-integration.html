Let \(X\) be a complex variety of dimension \(d\). For all \(m \ge 0\), denote by \[ J_m(X) = \Hom(\Spec \CC[t] / (t^{m + 1}), X) \] the \(m\)-th jet space of \(X\), and by \[ J_\infty(X) = \Hom(\Spec \CC \llbracket t \rrbracket, X) \] the formal arc space of \(X\). For all \(m \ge n\), there are natural truncation maps \[ \pi^m_n : J_m(X) \to J_n(X) \quad \textup{ and } \quad \pi_m : J_\infty(X) \to J_m(X) . \] A subset \(A \subset J_\infty(X)\) is called a cylinder if \(A = \pi^{-1}_m(C_m)\) for some \(m \ge 0\) and constructible set \(C_m \subset J_m(X)\).
Denote by \(\widehat{\mathcal{M}_\mathbb{L}}\) the completion of the localization \(\textup{K}_0(\textbf{Var}_\CC)[\mathbb{L}^{-1}]\) of the Grothendieck ring of varieties at the Lefschetz class \(\mathbb{L} = [\AA^1_\CC]\). The completion is taken with respect to the filtration by dimension.
For any cylinder \(A \subset J_\infty(X)\), one can show that the limit \[ \mu(A) = \lim_{m \to \infty} [\pi_m(A)] / \mathbb{L}^{d (m + 1)} \in \widehat{\mathcal{M}_\mathbb{L}} \] exists. The function \(\mu\) is called the motivic measure on the set of cylinders of \(J_\infty(X)\).
For any cylinder \(A \subset J_\infty(X)\) and function \(\alpha : A \to \ZZ \cup \{ \infty \}\) such that \(\alpha^{-1}(k) \subset J_\infty(X)\) are also cylinders for all \(k \ge 0\), the motivic integral of \(\mathbb{L}^{-\alpha}\) over \(A\) is defined as \[ \int_A \mathbb{L}^{- \alpha} d\mu = \sum_{k \in \ZZ} \mu(\alpha^{-1}(k)) \mathbb{L}^{-k} \in \widehat{\mathcal{M}_\mathbb{L}} \] whenever it exists.
