Let \(A = (a_{ij})\) be an \(n \times n\) matrix. The cofactor of an entry \(a_{ij}\) of \(A\) is \[ a'_{ij} = (-1)^{i + j} \det(A_{ij}), \] where \(A_{ij}\) is the matrix obtained from \(A\) by removing the \(i\)-th row and \(j\)-th column. The cofactor matrix of \(A\) is then the \(n \times n\) matrix \(A' = (a'_{ij})\).
