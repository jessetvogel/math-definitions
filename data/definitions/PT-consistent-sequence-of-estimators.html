Let \(X_1, X_2, \ldots\) be a random sample from a population \(F_\theta\) depending on some parameter \(\theta\) in a parameter space \(\Theta \subseteq \RR^N\). A sequence \[ W_1(X_1), W_2(X_1, X_2), \ldots, W_n(X_1, \ldots, X_n), \ldots \] of estimators for \(\theta\) is consistent if, for every \(\varepsilon > 0\) and every \(\theta \in \Theta\), \[ \lim_{n \to \infty} \PP_\theta(|W_n(X_1, \ldots, X_n) - \theta| < \varepsilon) = 1 . \]
