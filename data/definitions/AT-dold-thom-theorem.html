Let \(X\) be a connected CW-complex with basepoint \(x \in X\). The symmetric products \(\operatorname{SP}^n(X) = X^n / S_n\) admit embeddings \(\operatorname{SP}^n(X) \to \operatorname{SP}^{n + 1}(X)\) via \((x_1, \ldots, x_n) \mapsto (x_1, \ldots, x_n, x)\).
