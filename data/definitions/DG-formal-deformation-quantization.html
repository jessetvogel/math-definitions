Let \((M, \omega)\) be a symplectic manifold. A formal deformation quantization of \(M\) is an associative product \(\star\) on \(Z = C^\infty(M; \CC) \llbracket \hbar \rrbracket\), called a star product, bilinear over \(\CC \llbracket \hbar \rrbracket\), satisfying
  • (formal deformation) \(a \star b \mod \hbar = ab\) for all \(a, b \in Z\),
  • (locality) if \(a, b \in Z\), then \[ a \star b = \sum_{k \ge 0} \hbar^k c_k , \] with \(c_k\) depending only on \(\partial^\alpha a_i \partial^\beta b_j\) for \(i + j + |\alpha| + |\beta| \le k\).
  • (correspondence principle) if \(a, b \in Z\), then \[ [a, b] = a \star b - b \star a = - i \hbar \{ a_0, b_0 \} + \mathcal{O}(\hbar^2) , \] where \(\{ \cdot, \cdot \}\) denotes the Poisson bracket associated to \(\omega\).
