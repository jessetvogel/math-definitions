Let \(\mathfrak{S}\) be a site. A gerbe over \(\mathfrak{S}\) is a stack \(\pi : \mathfrak{X} \to \mathfrak{S}\) such that
  • for every object \(U\) of \(\mathfrak{S}\), there exists a covering \(\{ U_i \to U \}\) in \(\mathfrak{S}\) such that the fiber category \(\mathfrak{X}_{U_i}\) is non-empty,
  • for every object \(U\) of \(\mathfrak{S}\) and \(x, y \in \mathfrak{X}_U\), there exists a covering \(\{ U_i \to U \}\) in \(\mathfrak{S}\) such that \(x|_{U_i} \cong y|_{U_i}\) for all \(i\).
