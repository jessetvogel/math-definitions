Let \(M\) be a compact smooth manifold, and \(G\) a finite group acting on \(M\). The orbifold Euler characteristic of the orbifold \(M/G\) is \[ \chi^\textup{orb}(M/G) = \frac{1}{|G|} \sum_{\substack{g, h \in G \\ \textup{s.t. } gh = hg}} \chi(M^{g, h}) , \] where \(M^{g, h}\) denotes the submanifold of points fixed by both \(g\) and \(h\), and \(\chi\) denotes the ordinary Euler characteristic.
