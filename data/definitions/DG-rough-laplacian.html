Let \((M, g)\) be a Riemannian manifold, \(E \to M\) a vector bundle on \(M\) with connection \(\nabla\). The rough Laplacian on \(E\) is the operator \[ \Delta : \Gamma(E) \to \Gamma(E), \quad s \mapsto \operatorname{tr}(\nabla^2 s) \] where \(\nabla^2 s \in \Gamma(E \otimes T^*M \otimes T^*M) \cong \Gamma(E \otimes \operatorname{End}(TM))\) via the metric \(g\), and \(\operatorname{tr}\) takes the trace.
