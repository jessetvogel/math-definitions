Let \((M, \omega)\) be a symplectic manifold. A symplectic connection on \(M\) is a affine connection \(\nabla\) which is torsion-free and preserves the symplectic form, that is \[ (\nabla_Z \omega)(X, Y) = d(w(X, Y)) - \omega(\nabla_Z X, Y) - \omega(X, \nabla_Z Y) = 0 \] for any vector fields \(X, Y\) and \(Z\).
