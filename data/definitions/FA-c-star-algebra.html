A C*-algebra is a Banach algebra \(A\) over \(\CC\) together with a map \(A \to A, x \mapsto x^*\) satisfying
  • (involution) \((x^*)^* = x\) for all \(x \in A\),
  • (antiautomorphism) \((x + y)^* = x^* + y^*\) and \((xy)^* = y^* x^*\) for all \(x, y \in A\),
  • (\(\CC\)-antilinear) \((\lambda x)^* = \overline{\lambda} x^*\) for all \(\lambda \in \CC\) and \(x \in A\),
  • (C*-identity) \(\norm{x^* x} = \norm{x}^2\) for all \(x \in A\).
The first three conditions equivalent to saying \(A\) is a *-algebra.
