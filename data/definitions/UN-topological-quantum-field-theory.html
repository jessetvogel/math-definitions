Let \((\textbf{Bd}_n, \sqcup, \varnothing)\) be the monoidal category of (diffeomorphism classes of) \(n\)-dimensional (oriented) bordisms, and let \(k\) be a commutative ring. An \(n\)-dimensional Topological Quantum Field Theory (TQFT) over \(k\) is a monoidal functor \[ Z : \textbf{Bd}_n \to \textbf{Mod}_k , \] where the monoidal structure on \(\textbf{Mod}_k\) is given by the tensor product.
