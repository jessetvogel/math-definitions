The sign of a permutation \(\sigma \in S_n\) is defined as \[ \textup{sign}(\sigma) = \prod_{1 \le i < j \le n} \frac{\sigma(j) - \sigma(i)}{j - i} \in \{ +1, -1 \} . \] We call \(\sigma\) even if \(\textup{sign}(\sigma) = 1\) and odd if \(\textup{sign}(\sigma) = -1\).
This defines a group homomorphism \[ \textup{sign} : S_n \to \{ +1, -1 \} . \]
