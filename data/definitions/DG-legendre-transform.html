Let \(V\) be a real vector space and \(f : V \to \RR\) a smooth function which is super-linear, that is, \(\sup_{v \in V} \left(p(v) - f(v) \right) < \infty\) for all \(p \in V^*\). Then the Legendre transform of \(f\) is \[ f^* : V^* \to \RR, \quad p \mapsto \sup_{v \in V} \left(p(v) - f(v) \right) . \]
