Let \(X\) be a topological space. The cohomology of \(X\) with compact support \(H_c^n(X; R)\) is given by the cohomology of the following subcomplex of the singular cohomology complex \[ C_c^n(X; R) = \big\{ f : \mathcal{S}(X)_n \to R : \text{ there is a compact $K \subset X$ with $f(\sigma) = 0$ for all $\sigma \in \mathcal{S}(X \setminus K)_n$} \big\} . \]
Alternatively, \[ H_c^n(X; R) = \varinjlim H^n(X, X \setminus K; R) \] where the direct limit is taken over all compact \(K \subset X\) w.r.t. inclusions.
