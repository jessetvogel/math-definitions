Let \(M\) be a smooth manifold and \(f : M \to \RR\) a Morse function on \(M\). Morse lemma states that for every critical point \(p \in M\) of \(f\), there exists a chart \(\phi : U \to \RR^n\) around \(p\) such that \[ (f \circ \phi^{-1})(x_1, \ldots, x_n) = f(p) + \sum_{i = 1}^{k} x_i^2 - \sum_{i = k + 1}^{n} x_i^2 . \]
