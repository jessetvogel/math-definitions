Let \(X\) be a scheme. The Hilbert functor \(H_X : \textbf{Sch}^\textup{op} \to \textbf{Set}\) is given by \[ H_X(S) = \{ \text{$S$-flat closed subschemes $W \subset X \times S$} \} . \] The infinitesimal local version of this moduli functor for a given closed subscheme \(Z \subset X\) is the deformation functor \[ H_{X, Z}(A) = \left\{ \begin{array}{cc} \text{$(\Spec A)$-flat closed subschemes $Z' \subset X \times \Spec A$} \\ \text{whose fiber over $(\Spec A)_\textup{red}$ is $Z$} \end{array} \right\} . \]
