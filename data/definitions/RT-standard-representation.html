For any \(n \ge 1\) and \(k\) a field with \(n! \nmid \operatorname{char}(k)\), let \(\rho : S_n \to \textup{GL}_n(k)\) be the representation of the symmetric group \(S_n\) which permutes the basis vectors. The standard representation of \(S_n\) is the quotient of \(\rho\) by the one-dimensional subrepresentation spanned by the sum of the basis vectors. Alternatively, the standard representation of \(S_n\) is the subrepresentation of \(\rho\) corresponding to the subspace of \(k^n\) where the sum of coordinates is zero.
The standard representation is irreducible.
