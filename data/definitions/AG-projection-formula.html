Let \(f : X \to Y\) be a morphism of schemes, \(\mathcal{F}\) an \(\mathcal{O}_X\)-module, and \(\mathcal{E}\) a locally free \(\mathcal{O}_Y\)-module. The projection formula states a natural isomorphism \[ f_*\left(\mathcal{F} \otimes_{\mathcal{O}_X} f^* \mathcal{E} \right) \isom f_*\left(\mathcal{F}\right) \otimes_{\mathcal{O}_Y} \mathcal{E} . \]
