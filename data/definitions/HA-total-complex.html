Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category and \((A_{\bdot, \bdot}, d^h, d^v)\) a double complex in \(\mathcal{A}\). The total complex of \(A_{\bdot, \bdot}\) is the chain complex \((A_\bdot, d)\) with \[ A_n = \bigoplus_{i + j = n} A_{i, j} \] and \[ d_n = \bigoplus_{i + j = n} d^v_{i, j} + (-1)^j d^h_{i, j} : A_n \to A_{n - 1} . \]
