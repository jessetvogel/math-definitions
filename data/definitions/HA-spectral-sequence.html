Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category. A spectral sequence in \(\mathcal{A}\) is a collection of objects \[ (E_r^{p, q}, E^n) \qquad n, p, q, r \in \ZZ, r \ge 1 \] and morphisms \[ d_r^{p, q} : E_r^{p, q} \to E_r^{p + r, q - r + 1} \] satisfying
  • \(d_r^2 = 0\) (i.e. the \(E_r^{p + \bdot r, q - \bdot r + \bdot}\) are complexes),
  • there are isomorphisms (which are part of the data) \[ E_{r + 1}^{p, q} \isom H^0(E_r^{p + \bdot r, q - \bdot r + \bdot}), \]
  • for any \((p, q)\) there exists an \(r_0\) such that \(d_r^{p, q} = d_r^{p - r, q + r - 1} = 0\) for all \(r \ge r_0\). In particular, \(E_r^{p, q} \isom E_{r_0}^{p, q}\) for all \(r \ge r_0\), and this object is denoted by \(E_\infty^{p, q}\).
  • There is a decreasing filtration \[ 0 \subset \cdots \subset F^{p + 1} E^n \subset F^p E^n \subset \cdots E^n , \] with \(\cap_p F^p E^n = 0\) and \(\cup_p F^p E^n = E^n\), and isomorphisms \[ E_\infty^{p, q} \isom F^p E^{p + q} / F^{p + 1} E^{p + q} . \]

In some sense, the objects \(E_r^{p, q}\) converge towards subquotients of a certain filtration of \(E^n\). Usually, the objects of one layer of some fixed \(r\) are given. Then one writes \[ E_r^{p, q} \Rightarrow E^{p + q} . \]
