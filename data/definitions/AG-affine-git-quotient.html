Let \(G\) be a reductive algebraic group acting on an affine scheme \(X = \Spec R\). Then there is a dual action of \(G\) on \(R\) given by \(\widehat{\sigma} : R \to R \otimes \mathcal{O}_G(G)\), yielding the ring of invariants \[ R^G := \{ r \in R : \widehat{\sigma}(r) = r \otimes 1 \} . \] The affine GIT quotient of \(X\) by \(G\) is the morphism \[ \pi : X \to X \sslash G := \Spec\left(R^G\right) \] induced by the inclusion \(R^G \to R\).
