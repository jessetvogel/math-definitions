The Hopf fibration is a non-trivial fiber bundle over \(S^2\) with fiber \(S^1\), whose total space is \(S^3\). \[ S^1 \hookrightarrow S^3 \to S^2 \] Identifying \(S^3 \isom \{ (z_0, z_1) \in \CC^2 : |z_0|^2 + |z_1|^2 = 1 \}\) and \(S^2 \isom \CC P^1\), the Hopf fibration is given by \[ S^3 \to S^2, \quad (z_0, z_1) \mapsto (z_0 : z_1) . \]
