Let \(X\) be a scheme, and \(\mathcal{F}\) a coherent sheaf on \(X\). The Quot functor \(Q_\mathcal{F}\) associated to \(\mathcal{F}\) is given by \[ Q_\mathcal{F}(S) = \{ \text{coherent subsheaves } \mathcal{S} \subset p^* \mathcal{F} : p^* \mathcal{F} / \mathcal{S} \text{ is flat over } S \} , \] where \(p : X \times S \to S\) denotes the projection.
Note that the Hilbert functor is the special case where \(\mathcal{F} = \mathcal{O}_X\).
