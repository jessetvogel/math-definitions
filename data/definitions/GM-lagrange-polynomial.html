Given \(k + 1\) distinct numbers \(x_0, \ldots, x_k\), the Lagrange basis polynomials are the polynomials \[ \ell_j(x) = \prod_{\substack{0 \le i \le k \\ i \ne j}} \frac{x - x_i}{x_j - x_i} . \] Given \(k + 1\) values \(y_0, \ldots, y_k\), the Lagrange interpolating polynomial through the points \((x_0, y_0), \ldots, (x_k, y_k)\) is the polynomial \[ L(x) = \sum_{j = 0}^{k} y_j \ell_j(x) . \]
