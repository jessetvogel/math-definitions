Let \(R\) be a commutative ring, and let \(M\) and \(N\) be \(R\)-modules. The tensor product of \(M\) and \(N\) is an \(R\)-module, denoted \(M \otimes_R N\), together with a bilinear map \(\varphi : M \times N \to M \otimes_R N\) having the universal property that every bilinear map \(\psi : M \times N \to L\) factors uniquely through \(\varphi\).
