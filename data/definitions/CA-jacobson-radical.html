The Jacobson radical of a commutative ring \(R\) is the ideal given by the intersection of all maximal ideals, \[ \mathfrak{J}_R = \bigcap_{\mathfrak{m} \subset R \text{ maximal}} \mathfrak{m} . \] It can also be characterized by \[ x \in \mathfrak{J}_R \iff 1 - xy \text{ is a unit for all } y \in R . \]
