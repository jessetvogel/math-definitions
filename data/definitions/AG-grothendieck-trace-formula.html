Let \(X\) be a variety over a finite field \(\FF_q\), and let \(\overline{X} = X \times_{\FF_q} \overline{\FF}_q\) for an algebraic closure \(\overline{\FF}_q\) of \(\FF_q\). Let \(F : \overline{X} \to \overline{X}\) be the morphism induced by the Frobenius morphism \(\overline{\FF}_q \to \overline{\FF}_q\) given by \(x \mapsto x^q\). The Grothendieck trace formula states that \[ \# X(\FF_q) = \sum_{i = 0}^{2 \dim X} \tr(F, H^i_c(\overline{X}, \QQ_\ell)) , \] where \(F\) naturally acts on the (compactly supported) \(\ell\)-adic cohomology of \(\overline{X}\).
