Let \(X\) be a topological space with an equivalence relation \(\sim{}\), denote the set of equivalence classes by \(X / \sim{}\), and let \(\pi : X \to X / \sim{}\) be the natural map. The quotient topology on \(X / \sim{}\) is given by \[ \tilde{\mathcal{T}} = \{ U \subset X / \sim{} : \pi^{-1}(U) \text{ open in } X \} . \] This is the finest topology that makes \(\pi\) continuous.
