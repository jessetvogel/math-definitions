Let \(M\) be a smooth manifold. A Morse function on \(M\) is a smooth function \(f : M \to \RR\) such that for every critical point \(p \in M\) of \(f\), there exists a chart \(\phi : U \to \RR^n\) around \(p\) such that the Hessian matrix \[ \left( \frac{\partial^2 (f \circ \phi^{-1})}{\partial x_i \partial x_j} (\phi(p)) \right)_{i, j = 1, \ldots, n} \] is invertible.
