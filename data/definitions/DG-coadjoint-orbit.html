Let \(G\) be a Lie group and \(\mathfrak{g}\) its corresponding Lie algebra. The coadjoint orbit of some \(\xi \in \mathfrak{g}^*\) is the orbit of the coadjoint representation \(\textup{Ad}^*_G : G \to \textup{GL}(\mathfrak{g}^*)\), that is \[ \mathcal{O}_\xi = \{ \textup{Ad}_g^*(\xi) : g \in G \} . \] Equivalently, it can be described as the quotient \(G/G_\xi\), where \(G_\xi\) is the stabilizer of \(\xi\), under the isomorphism \[ G/G_\xi \xrightarrow{\sim} \mathcal{O}_\xi, \quad g \mapsto \textup{Ad}_g^*(\mu) . \]
