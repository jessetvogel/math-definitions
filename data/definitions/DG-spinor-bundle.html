Let \(P\) be a \(\textup{Spin}(n)\)-structure, with \(n\) even, on a Riemannian manifold \((M, g)\). The associated spinor bundle is the associated bundle \[ S_P = P \times_{\textup{Spin}(n)} \Delta_n \to M \] where \(\Delta_n \cong \CC^{2^{n/2}}\) is acted on via \(\textup{Spin}(n) \hookrightarrow \textup{Cl}_n \hookrightarrow \textup{Cl}_n \otimes \CC \cong \operatorname{End}(\CC^{2^{n/2}})\).
