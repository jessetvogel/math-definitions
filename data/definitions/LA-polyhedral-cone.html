A polyhedral cone in \(\RR^n\) is a set of the form \[ \sigma = \{ \lambda_1 v_1 + \cdots + \lambda_r v_r : \lambda_i \in \RR_{\ge 0} , \] for some \(v_1, \ldots, v_r \in \RR^n\). The vectors \(v_1, \ldots, v_r\) are called generators of the cone \(\sigma\).
The dimension of \(\sigma\) is the dimension of the linear subspace spanned by \(\sigma\).
