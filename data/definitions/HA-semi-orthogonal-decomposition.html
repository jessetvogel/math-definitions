Let \(\mathcal{D}\) be a triangulated category. A sequence of full admissible triangulated subcategories \(\mathcal{D}_1, \mathcal{D}_2, \ldots, \mathcal{D}_n \subset \mathcal{D}\) is semi-orthogonal if \[ \mathcal{D}_i \subset \mathcal{D}_j^\perp \qquad \text{for all $i < j$}. \] Such a sequence defines a semi-orthogonal decomposition of \(\mathcal{D}\) if \(\mathcal{D}\) is generated by the \(\mathcal{D}_i\). In this case one writes \[ \mathcal{D} = \langle \mathcal{D}_1, \mathcal{D}_2, \ldots, \mathcal{D}_n \rangle . \]
