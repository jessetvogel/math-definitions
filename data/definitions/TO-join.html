The join of two topological spaces \(X\) and \(Y\) is the quotient space \[ X \star Y = (X \times Y \times [0, 1]) / \sim{} , \] where the equivalence relation is generated by \[ (x, y_1, 0) \sim{} (x, y_2, 0) \text{ for all } x \in X \text{ and } y_1, y_2 \in Y , \] \[ (x_1, y, 1) \sim{} (x_2, y, 1) \text{ for all } x_1, x_2 \in X \text{ and } y \in Y . \]
