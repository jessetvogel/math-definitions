Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be a \(\sigma\)-algebra. A measure on \(\mathcal{A}\) is a function \(\mu : \mathcal{A} \to [0, \infty]\) satisfying
  • (measure empty set) \(\mu(\varnothing) = 0\),
  • (countable additivity) \(\mu(\bigcup_{n = 1}^\infty A_n) = \sum_{n = 1}^\infty \mu(A_n)\) for all \(A_n \in \mathcal{A}\) with \(n \in \NN\) pairwise disjoint.
