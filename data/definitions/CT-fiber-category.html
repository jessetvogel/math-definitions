Let \(\mathfrak{S}\) be a category and \(\pi : \mathfrak{X} \to \mathfrak{S}\) a category over \(\mathfrak{X}\). The fiber category \(\mathfrak{X}_S\) of an object \(S\) of \(\mathfrak{S}\) is the subcategory of \(\mathfrak{X}\) of all objects \(x\) with \(\pi(x) = S\) and morphisms \(\alpha : x \to y\) with \(\pi(\alpha) = \id_S\).
