Let \(X\) be a topological space, and let \(\textup{Aut}(X)\) be the topological group of automorphisms of \(X\), whose topology is given by the compact-open topology. Then the mapping class group of \(X\) is the quotient \[ \textup{MCG}(X) = \textup{Aut}(X) / \textup{Aut}_0(X) , \] where \(\textup{Aut}_0(X)\) is the path-component of the identity in \(\textup{Aut}(X)\). Note that \(f, g \in \textup{Aut}(X)\) define the same element in \(\textup{MCG}(X)\) if and only if they are isotopic.
