Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category. A double complex \(A_{\bdot, \bdot}\) in \(\mathcal{A}\) is a sequence of objects \(A_{i, j}\) in \(\mathcal{A}\), for \(i, j \in \ZZ\), together with horizontal and vertical morphisms \(d^h_{i, j} : A_{i, j} \to A_{i - 1, j}\) and \(d^v_{i, j} : A_{i, j} \to A_{i, j - 1}\), satisfying \[ (d^h)^2 = (d^v)^2 = d^h d^v - d^v d^h = 0 . \]
