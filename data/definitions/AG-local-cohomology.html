Let \(X\) be a topological space and let \(i : A \to X\) be the inclusion of a closed subset. Consider the functor \[ \Gamma_A(X, -) : \textup{Sh}(X) \to \textbf{Ab}, \quad \mathcal{F} \mapsto \Gamma_A(X, \mathcal{F}) = \{ s \in \Gamma(X, \mathcal{F}) \;|\;\textup{Supp}(s) \subset A \} \] of global sections with support in \(A\). This is a left exact functor, and one can form the right derived functors \(H_A^i(X, -) = \textup{R}^i \Gamma_A(X, -)\). The group \[ H_A^i(X, \mathcal{F}) \] is called the \(i\)-th local cohomology group of \(X\) with support in \(A\) and coefficients in \(\mathcal{F}\).
