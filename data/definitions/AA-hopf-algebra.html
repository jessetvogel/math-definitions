A Hopf algebra over a field \(k\) is a bialgebra \(A\) over \(k\) together with a \(k\)-linear map \(S : A \to A\) (the antipode), such that the diagram commutes, where \(\mu\) denotes the multiplication, \(\Delta\) the comultiplication, \(\eta\) the unit, and \(\varepsilon\) the counit of the algebra.
