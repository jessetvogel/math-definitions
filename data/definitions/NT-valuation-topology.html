Let \(K\) be a field with valuation \(\phi : K \to \RR_{\ge 0}\). The valuation topology on \(K\) is the topology \(\mathcal{T}_\phi\) on \(K\) for which a basis is given by the open balls \[ U_\varepsilon(x) = \{ y \in K \mid \phi(x - y) < \varepsilon \} \] for all \(x \in K\) and \(\varepsilon > 0\).
