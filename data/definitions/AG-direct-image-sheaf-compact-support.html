Let \(f : X \to Y\) be a map of topological spaces, and let \(\mathcal{F}\) be a sheaf on \(X\). The direct image sheaf with compact support \(f_! \mathcal{F}\) on \(Y\) is defined by \[ (f_! \mathcal{F})(V) = \{ s \in \mathcal{F}(f^{-1}(V)) \mid f_{\operatorname{supp}(s)} : \operatorname{supp}(s) \to V \textup{ is proper} \} \] for any open set \(V \subset Y\) (indeed this presheaf is a sheaf).
