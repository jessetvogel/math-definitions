A variety \(X\) over a field \(k\) is nonsingular at a point \(x \in X\) if the local ring \(\mathcal{O}_{X, x}\) is regular. The variety \(X\) is nonsingular if it is nonsingular at all points \(x \in X\).
Equivalently, \(X\) is nonsingular if and only if the structure morphism \(X \to \Spec k\) is smooth.
