Let \(X, Y \to S\) be schemes, \(\mathcal{F}\) an \(\mathcal{O}_X\)-module and \(\mathcal{G}\) an \(\mathcal{O}_Y\)-module. The external tensor product of \(\mathcal{F}\) and \(\mathcal{G}\) is the \(\mathcal{O}_{X \times_S Y}\)-module \[ \mathcal{F} \boxtimes \mathcal{G} = \pi_X^* \mathcal{F} \otimes_{\mathcal{O}_S} \pi_Y^* \mathcal{G} . \]
