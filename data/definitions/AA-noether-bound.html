Let \(G\) be a finite group and \(\rho : G \to \textup{GL}(V)\) a finite-dimensional representation over a field \(k\). The action of \(G\) naturally extends to the polynomial ring \(k[x_1, \ldots, x_n]\), where \(n = \dim_k V\).
