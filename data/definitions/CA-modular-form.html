Let \(\Gamma \subset \textup{SL}_2(\ZZ)\) be a subgroup of finite index and \(k\) an integer. A modular form of level \(\Gamma\) and weight \(k\) is a holomorphic function \(f : \mathbb{H} \to \CC\) on the upper half-plane \(\mathbb{H} = \{ z \in \CC \mid \operatorname{Im}(z) > 0 \}\) satisfying
  • \(f\left(\frac{az + b}{cz + d}\right) = (cz + d)^k f(z)\) for all \(z \in \mathbb{H}\) and \(\begin{pmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{pmatrix} \in \Gamma\),
  • the function \((cz + d)^{-k} f\left(\frac{az + b}{cz + d}\right)\) is bounded as \(\operatorname{Im}(z) \to \infty\), for all \(\begin{pmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{pmatrix} \in \textup{SL}_2(\ZZ)\).
