Let \(M\) be a smooth manifold, \(G\) a Lie group acting on \(M\) via \(\varphi : G \times M \to M\). For any \(\xi \in \mathfrak{g}\) in the Lie algebra of \(G\), the fundamental vector field of \(\xi\) is the vector field on \(M\) given by \[ X_\xi(p) = d(\varphi(-, p))(e) \xi \in T_p M . \] Alternatively, we have \[ X_\xi(p) = \frac{d}{d t}\Big|_{t = 0} \varphi(\exp(t \xi), p) . \]
