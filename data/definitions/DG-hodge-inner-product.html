Let \((M, g)\) be a Riemannian manifold. The Hodge inner product is the (global) inner product on the de Rham spaces \(\Omega^k(M)\) given by \[ (\alpha, \beta) = \int_X \alpha \wedge \star \beta = \int_X g(\alpha, \beta) \; \textup{vol}_g \] for \(\alpha, \beta \in \Omega^k(M)\), where \(\star\) denotes the Hodge star operator.
