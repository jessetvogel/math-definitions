Let \(M\) be a smooth manifold. A vector bundle of rank \(k\) over \(M\) is a smooth manifold \(E\) together with a surjective smooth map \(\pi : E \to M\) such that
  • for each \(p \in M\), the fiber \(E_p = \pi^{-1}(p)\) has the structure of a real vector space,
  • for each \(p \in M\), there exists a neighborhood \(U \subset M\) of \(p\) and a diffeomorphism \(\Phi : \pi^{-1}(U) \to U \times \RR^k\) such that commutes, and the restriction of \(\Phi|_{E_p} : E_p \to \{ p \} \times \RR^k \simeq \RR^k\) is a linear isomorphism.
