Let \(f : R \to S\) be morphism of commutative rings. Extension of scalars along \(f\) is the functor \[ (-) \otimes_R S : R\textup{-}\textbf{Mod} \to S\textup{-}\textbf{Mod} \] assigning to an \(R\)-module \(M\) the tensor product \(M \otimes_R S\).
Extension of scalars is left adjoint to restriction of scalars.
