Let \(\nabla\) be a connection on a vector bundle \(\pi : E \to M\). The curvature form of \(E\) is the \(2\)-form \(k(E) = \frac{1}{2 \pi i} \tr F_\nabla \in \Omega^2(M)\), where \(F_\nabla \in \Omega^2(M; \textup{End}(E))\) is the curvature of \(\nabla\). It is a closed form and the corresponding cohomology class \(\kappa(E) = [k(E)] \in H_\textup{dR}^2(M)\) is called the curvature class of \(E\).
