An algebraic torus \(T\) over a field \(k\) is an algebraic group which, over an algebraic closure \(\overline{k}\) of \(k\), is isomorphic to a finite product of copies of \(\mathbb{G}_m = \Spec \overline{k}[t, t^{-1}]\), that is \[ T \times_k \overline{k} \simeq \Spec \overline{k}[t_1, t_1^{-1}, \ldots, t_r, t_r^{-1}] , \] where \(r\) is called the rank of the algebraic torus \(T\).
