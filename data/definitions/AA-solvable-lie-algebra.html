A Lie algebra \(\mathfrak{g}\) is solvable if the derived series of subalgebras \[ \mathfrak{g} = \mathfrak{g}^{(0)} \supset \mathfrak{g}^{(1)} \supset \mathfrak{g}^{(2)} \supset \cdots \] given by \(\mathfrak{g}^{(i + 1)} = [\mathfrak{g}^{(i)}, \mathfrak{g}^{(i)}]\), results in the zero algebra. That is, \(\mathfrak{g}^{(i)} = \{ 0 \}\) for some \(i > 0\).
