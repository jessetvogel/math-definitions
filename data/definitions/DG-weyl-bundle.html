Let \((M, \omega)\) be a symplectic manifold. The Weyl bundle on \(M\) is the vector bundle \[ W = \widehat{\textup{Sym}}(T^*M) , \] that is, the completed symmetric algebra of the cotangent bundle.
The formal Weyl algebra is the vector bundle \[ W_\hbar = \widehat{\textup{Sym}}(T^*M) \llbracket \hbar \rrbracket , \] where \(\hbar\) is a formal parameter.
