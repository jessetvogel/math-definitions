An adic ring is a topological ring \(A\) for which there exists an ideal \(I \subset A\), called the ideal of definition, such that \(\{ I^n : n \in \NN \}\) is a neighborhood basis of \(0\) in \(A\).
A Huber ring is a topological ring \(A\) for which there exists an open adic subring \(A_0 \subset A\) with finitely generated ideal of definition \(I\).
