Let \((\mathfrak{g}_\bdot, d, [-, -])\) be a differential graded Lie algebra. The Maurer–Cartan equation in \(\mathfrak{g}_\bdot\) is the equation \[ da + \frac{1}{2} [a, a] = 0, \quad a \in \mathfrak{g}_{-1} . \] A Maurer–Cartan element of \(\mathfrak{g}_\bdot\) is an element \(a \in \mathfrak{g}_{-1}\) satisfying the Maurer–Cartan equation.
