Let \(\pi : E \to M\) be a vector bundle over a smooth manifold \(M\), and \(\nabla\) a connection on \(E\). By parallel transport, any loop \(\gamma : [0, 1] \to M\) based at a point \(x \in M\) yields a linear invertible map \(P_\gamma : E_x \to E_x\). The holonomy group of \(\nabla\) based at \(x\) is the group \[ \textup{Hol}_x(\nabla) = \{ P_\gamma \in \textup{GL}(E_x) : \gamma \text{ is a loop based at } x \} . \] The restricted holonomy group is the subgroup \(\textup{Hol}_x^0(\nabla)\) coming from contractible loops.
