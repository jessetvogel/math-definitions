The Grothendieck group \(\textup{K}_0(\mathcal{A})\) of an additive category \(\mathcal{A}\) is defined as the free abelian group on isomorphism classes of objects in \(\mathcal{A}\), modulo the relations \([B] = [A] + [C]\) for every short exact sequence \[ 0 \to A \to B \to C \to 0 \] in \(\mathcal{A}\).
