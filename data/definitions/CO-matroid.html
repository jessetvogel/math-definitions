A (finite) matroid is a finite set \(E\), called the ground set, together with a set \(I\) of subsets of \(E\), known as the independent subsets, such that
  • \(\varnothing \in I\),
  • if \(B \in I\) and \(A \subset B\), then \(A \in I\),
  • if \(A, B \in I\) and \(|B| > |A|\), then there exists an \(b \in B\) such that \(A \cup \{ b \} \in I\).
