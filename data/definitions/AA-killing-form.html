Let \(\mathfrak{g}\) be a Lie algebra of finite dimension over a field \(k\). The Killing form of \(\mathfrak{g}\) is the symmetric bilinear form \[ \kappa_\mathfrak{g} : \mathfrak{g} \times \mathfrak{g} \to k, \quad \kappa_\mathfrak{g}(x, y) = \textup{tr}(\textup{ad}_{\mathfrak{g}}(x) \circ \textup{ad}_{\mathfrak{g}}(y)) \] where \(\textup{ad}_\mathfrak{g} : \mathfrak{g} \to \mathfrak{gl}(\mathfrak{g})\) is the adjoint representation of \(\mathfrak{g}\).
