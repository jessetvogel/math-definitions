Given a commutative ring \(R\) and ideal \(I\), the quotient ring \(R/I\) is the ring \[ R/I = \{ a + I : a \in R \} \] where addition and multiplication are given by \[ (a + I) + (b + I) = (a + b) + I \quad \textup{ and } \quad (a + I) (b + I) = (ab) + I . \] It has the universal property that each morphism of rings \(f : R \to S\) with \(f(I) = 0\) uniquely extends to a morphism \(R/I \to S\).
