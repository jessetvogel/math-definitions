Let \(K\) be a global field. The idele group of \(K\) is the group of units of the adele ring of \(K\), denoted \[ \mathbb{I}_K = \mathbb{A}_K^* . \] The idele group is a topological group considered as \(\{ (x, x^{-1}) \in \mathbb{A}_K \times \mathbb{A}_K : x \in \mathbb{I}_K \}\) with the subspace topology.
