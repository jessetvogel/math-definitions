Let \(M\) be a smooth manifold. Given a rank \(k\) distribution \(\mathcal{D} \subset TM\) and a point \(p \in M\), an integral submanifold of \(\mathcal{D}\) through \(p\) is an immersion of a \(k\)-dimensional manifold \(i : N \to M\) such that \(p \in i(N)\) and for all \(q \in N\) one has \(i_*(T_q N) = \mathcal{D}_{i(q)}\).
