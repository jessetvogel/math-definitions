A Galois category is a category \(\mathcal{C}\) with a functor \(F : \mathcal{C} \to \textbf{FSet}\) to the category of finite sets, such that
  • \(\mathcal{C}\) has finite (co)limits,
  • any morphism \(f : X \to Y\) in \(\mathcal{C}\) can be written as \(f = m \circ e\) with \(e\) an epimorphism and \(m\) a monomorphism onto a direct summand of \(Y\),
  • \(F\) preserves finite (co)limits and reflects isomorphisms.
