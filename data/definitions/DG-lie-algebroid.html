Let \(M\) be a smooth manifold. A Lie algebroid over \(M\) is a vector bundle \(L\) on \(M\) together with a Lie bracket \([\cdot, \cdot]\) on its global sections \(\Gamma(L)\) and a map of vector bundles \(a : L \to TM\), called the anchor map, to the tangent bundle of \(M\), satisfying
  • (Lie algebra homomorphism) \(a([X, Y]) = [a(X), a(Y)]\) for all \(X, Y \in \Gamma(L)\),
  • (Leibniz rule) \([X, fY] = f[X, Y] + (a(X) f) Y\) for all \(X, Y \in \Gamma(L)\) and \(f \in C^\infty(M)\).
