Let \(\textbf{Mfld}_n^\textup{fr}\) be the symmetric monoidal \(\infty\)-category of framed manifolds under the disjoint union operator, whose \(1\)-morphisms are given by embeddings, whose \(2\)-morphisms are given by isotopies, etc. Let \(\textbf{Disk}_n\) be the full subcategory of \(\textbf{Mfld}_n^\textup{fr}\) consisting of disjoint unions of \(n\)-disks. An \(E_n\)-algebra \(\mathcal{E}\) of a symmetric monoidal \(\infty\)-category \(\mathcal{C}\) can be regarded as a functor \(\mathcal{E} : \textbf{Disk}_n \to \mathcal{C}\), and the factorization homology with coefficients in \(\mathcal{E}\) is defined as the left Kan extension of \(\mathcal{E}\) along the inclusion \(i : \textbf{Disk}_n \to \textbf{Mfld}_n^\textup{fr}\), also denoted by \(\int_{(-)} \mathcal{E}\).
