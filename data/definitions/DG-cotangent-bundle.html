Let \(M\) be an \(n\)-dimensional smooth manifold. The cotangent bundle of \(M\), denoted \(T^*M\), is defined as the disjoint union of the cotangent spaces at all points of \(M\), \[ T^*M = \bigsqcup_{p \in M} T^*_p M . \] It naturally has the structure of a \(2n\)-dimensional smooth manifold such that the projection map \[ \pi : T^*M \to M, \quad (p, \xi) \mapsto p \] is smooth.
