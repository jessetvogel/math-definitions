Let \(\pi : E \to M\) be a vector bundle of rank \(n\), and \(\nabla\) a connection on \(E\) with curvature \(F_\nabla \in \Omega^2(M; \textup{End}(E))\). The total Chern form of \(E\) is given by \[ C(E) = 1 + c_1(E) + \cdots + c_n(E) = \det \left(1 + \frac{F_\nabla}{2 \pi i} \right) , \] where \(c_k(E) \in \Omega^{2k}(M)\) is the \(k\)-th Chern form. The Chern forms are closed, and the cohomology class \([c_k(E)] \in H^{2k}(M)\) is called the \(k\)-th Chern class of \(E\). These classes are independent on the choice of connection.
