Let \(F : \mathcal{A} \to \mathcal{B}\) and \(G : \mathcal{B} \to \mathcal{C}\) be left exact functors between abelian categories, such that \(\mathcal{A}\) and \(\mathcal{B}\) have enough injectives, and \(F\) maps injective objects to \(G\)-acyclic objects. Then there is a spectral sequence \[ E_2^{p, q} = (R^p G \circ R^q F)(A) \Rightarrow R^{p + q}(G \circ F)(A) , \] called the Grothendieck spectral sequence.
