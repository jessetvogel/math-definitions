Let \(M\) be a smooth manifold. A Hermitian vector bundle on \(M\) is a complex vector bundle \(E \to M\) with a section \(h \in \Gamma((E \otimes \overline{E})^*)\), called a Hermitian metric, such that \(h_p\) is an inner product on \(E_p\) for all \(p \in M\).
One often writes \(\langle s, t \rangle = h(s, t)\) for \(s, t \in \Gamma(E)\).
