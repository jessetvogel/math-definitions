Let \(S = \bigoplus_{i \ge 0} S_i\) be a finitely generated graded commutative algebra over a field \(k\), with \(S_0 = k\). The Hilbert function \[ i \mapsto \dim_k S_i \] becomes polynomial for large enough values of \(i\), and the resulting polynomial is called the Hilbert polynomial \(\textup{HP}_S(i)\) of \(S\).
