Let \(M\) be a complex manifold with coordinates \(z^1, \ldots, z^n\). The Dolbeault operators are the operators \[ \partial = \sum_{j = 1}^{n} dz^j \wedge \frac{\partial}{\partial z^j} \quad \text{and} \quad \bar{\partial} = \sum_{j = 1}^{n} d\bar{z}_j \wedge \frac{\partial}{\partial \bar{z}_j} . \] They satisfy the properties \[ \partial + \bar{\partial} = d, \quad \partial^2 = \bar{\partial}^2 = \partial \bar{\partial} + \bar{\partial} \partial = 0 , \] where \(d\) is the exterior derivative.
