Let \(X\) be a scheme. The sheaf of rational functions \(K_X\) on \(X\) is the sheafification of the presheaf which assigns to an open \(U \subset X\) the localization \(S^{-1} \mathcal{O}_X(U)\), where \(S\) is the multiplicative set of all \(f \in \mathcal{O}_X(U)\) which are stalk-wise not zero-divisors.
