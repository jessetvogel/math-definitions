Let \(X\) be a scheme over a field \(k\). The group of algebraic \(r\)-cycles on \(X\) is the free abelian group on \(r\)-dimensional closed integral subschemes \(Z\) of \(X\), \[ Z_r(X) = \bigoplus_{Z \subset X} \ZZ \cdot [Z] . \] The group of algebraic cycles is the graded group \[ Z(X) = \bigoplus_{r \ge 0} Z_r(X) . \] An algebraic cycle \(\sum_i n_i [Z_i] \in Z(X)\) is effective if \(n_i \ge 0\) for all \(i\).
