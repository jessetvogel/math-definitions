Let \(X \subset \AA^n_k\) be an affine variety, and \(Z \subset X\) a closed subvariety given by the vanishing locus of \(f_1, \ldots, f_r \in \mathcal{O}_X(X)\). Writing \[ f : (X \setminus Z) \to \PP^{r - 1}, \quad x \mapsto (f_1(x) : \cdots : f_r(x)) , \] the blowup of \(X\) at \(Z\) is the closure of the graph \(\Gamma_f \subset X \times \PP^{r - 1}\), \[ \pi : \textup{Bl}_Z(X) := \overline{\Gamma_f} \to X . \]
