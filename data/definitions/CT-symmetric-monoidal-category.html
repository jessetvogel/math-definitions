A symmetric monoidal category is a monoidal category \(\mathcal{C}\) together with isomorphisms \[ \tau_{X, Y} : X \otimes Y \to Y \otimes X , \] natural in \(X\) and \(Y\), such that \[ \tau_{Y, X} \circ \tau_{X, Y} = \id_{X \otimes Y} \] and the diagrams and commute for all \(X, Y, Z\) in \(\mathcal{C}\).
