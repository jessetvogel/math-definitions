A \(\sigma\)-algebra on a set \(\Omega\) is a collection \(\mathcal{A}\) of subsets of \(\Omega\), satisfying
  • \(\Omega \in \mathcal{A}\),
  • \(\Omega \setminus A \in \mathcal{A}\) for all \(A \in \mathcal{A}\),
  • \(\bigcup_{n = 1}^\infty A_n \in \mathcal{A}\) for all sequences \((A_n)_{n \in \NN} \in \mathcal{A}\).
