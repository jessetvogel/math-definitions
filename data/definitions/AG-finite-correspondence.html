Let \(k\) be a field. For any schemes \(X\) and \(Y\) over \(k\), the group of finite correspondences from \(X\) to \(Y\) is the subgroup \[ C(X, Y) \subset Z(X \times_k Y) \] of algebraic cycles generated by integral closed subschemes \(W \subset X \times_k Y\) such that \(\pi_X : W \to X\) is finite and \(\pi_X(W) \subset X\) is an irreducible component of \(X\).
The category of finite correspondences over \(k\) is the category \(\textup{FinCorr}(k)\) whose objects are smooth schemes over \(k\), and whose morphisms from \(X\) to \(Y\) are given by \(C(X, Y)\).
