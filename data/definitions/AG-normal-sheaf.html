Let \(X\) be a non-singular variety over \(k\), and \(Y\) a non-singular closed irreducible subvariety defined by the sheaf of ideals \(\mathcal{I}\). The conormal sheaf of \(Y\) in \(X\) is the sheaf \[ \mathcal{I}/\mathcal{I}^2 . \] Its dual \[ \mathcal{N}_{Y/X} = \mathcal{H}\textup{om}(\mathcal{I}/\mathcal{I}^2, \mathcal{O}_X) \] is called the normal sheaf of \(Y\) in \(X\). It is locally free of rank \(r = \textup{codim}(Y)\).
