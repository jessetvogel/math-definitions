Let \(V\) be a vector space over a field \(k\), and \(S \subset V\) a subset. Then Steinitz exchange lemma states that, if \(v \in \textup{span}(S)\) with \(v \not\in \textup{span}(S \setminus \{ w \})\) for some \(w \in S\), then \[ \textup{span}(S) = \textup{span}((S \setminus \{ w \}) \cup \{ v \}) . \]
