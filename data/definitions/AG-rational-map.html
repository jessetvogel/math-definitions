A rational map \(f : X \dashrightarrow Y\) between (irreducible) varieties \(X\) and \(Y\) is an equivalence class of pairs \((U, f_U)\), where \(U \subset X\) open and non-empty, \(f_U : U \to Y\) a morphism, and where two pairs \((U, f_U)\) and \((V, f_V)\) are equivalent if their restriction to \(U \cap V\) coincides.
