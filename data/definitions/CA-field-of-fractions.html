The field of fractions of a domain \(R\) is the field given by \[ K = \left\{ (a, b) : a, b \in R, b \ne 0 \right\} / \sim{} \] where \((a, b) \sim{} (c, d)\) iff \(ad = bc\). Indeed \((a, b)\) represents the fraction \(\frac{a}{b}\). Addition and multiplication are given by \[ \frac{a}{b} + \frac{c}{d} = \frac{ad + bc}{bd} \quad \text{and} \quad \frac{a}{b} \cdot \frac{c}{d} = \frac{ac}{bd} . \]
