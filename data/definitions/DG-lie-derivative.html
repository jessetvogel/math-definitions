Let \(M\) be a smooth manifold. The Lie derivative of a tensor field \(T\) with respect to a vector field \(X\) is the tensor field \(\mathcal{L}_X T\) (of the same type as \(T\)), given by \[ (\mathcal{L}_X T)_p = \frac{d}{dt}\Big|_{t = 0} \left( \left(\varphi^t_X\right)^* T \right)_p \] for all points \(p \in M\). Here \(\varphi^t_X\) denotes the flow of \(X\).
