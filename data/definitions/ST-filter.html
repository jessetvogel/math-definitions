Let \((P, \le)\) be a partially ordered set. A filter on \(P\) is a subset \(F \subset P\) such that
  • (non-empty) \(F\) is non-empty,
  • (downward directed) for every \(x, y \in F\) there exists some \(z \in F\) with \(z \le x\) and \(z \le y\),
  • (upward-closed) for all \(x \in F\) and \(y \in P\) with \(x \le y\), also \(y \in F\).
An ultrafilter on \(P\) is a filter \(F \subsetneq P\) such that there exists no filter \(F'\) with \(F \subsetneq F' \subsetneq P\).
