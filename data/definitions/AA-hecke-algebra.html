Let \((W, S)\) be a finite Coxeter group. For a complex number \(q \in \CC\), the \(q\)-Hecke algebra \(\mathcal{H}_q(W, S)\) of \((W, S)\) is the \(\CC\)-algebra generated by \(\{ T_{s_i} \}_{s_i \in S}\) subject to the relations \[ T_{s_i}^2 = (q - 1) T_{s_i} + q \quad \textup{ and } \quad (T_{s_i} T_{s_j})^{m(s_i, s_j)} = 1 \textup{ for } i \ne j . \]
