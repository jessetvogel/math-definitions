Let \(f : R \to S\) be morphism of commutative rings. Coextension of scalars along \(f\) is the functor \[ \Hom_R(S, -) : \textbf{Mod}_R \to \textbf{Mod}_S \] where \(S\) is seen as an \(R\)-module via \(f\). The \(S\)-module structure on \(\Hom_R(S, M)\), for an \(R\)-module \(M\), is given by \((s \cdot \varphi)(x) = \varphi(s \cdot x)\) for all \(s, x \in S\) and \(\varphi \in \Hom_R(S, M)\).
Coextension of scalars is right adjoint to restriction of scalars.
