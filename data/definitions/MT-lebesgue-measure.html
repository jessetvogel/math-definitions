The Lebesgue outer measure is the outer measure \(\mu^*\) on \(\RR^n\) given by \[ \mu^*(A) = \inf \left\{ \sum_{n = 1}^{\infty} \ell(R_i) \;:\; R_i \subset \RR^n \textup{ closed rectangles}, A \subset \bigcup_{i = 1}^{\infty} R_i \right\} \in [0, \infty] , \] for all \(A \subset \RR^n\), where a closed rectangle is a product of closed intervals \(R_i = [a_1, b_1] \times \cdots \times [a_n, b_n]\) with volume \(\ell(R_i) = (b_1 - a_1) \cdots (b_n - a_n)\).
