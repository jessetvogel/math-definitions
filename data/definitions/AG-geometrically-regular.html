Let \(X\) be a locally noetherian scheme over a field \(k\). Then \(X\) is geometrically regular at \(x \in X\) over \(k\) if for every finitely generated field extension \(k \subset k'\) and any \(x' \in X_{k'}\) lying over \(x\), the local ring \(\mathcal{O}_{X_{k'}, x'}\) is regular.
The scheme \(X\) is geometrically regular over \(k\) if \(X\) it is so at all points \(x \in X\).
