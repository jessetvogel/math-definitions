Let \(C_\bdot\) and \(D_\bdot\) be chain complexes with values in an additive category \(\mathcal{A}\). The mapping complex associated to \(C_\bdot\) and \(D_\bdot\) is the chain complex \([C, D] _\bdot\) given by \[ [C, D]_n = \prod_{k \in \ZZ} \Hom_\mathcal{A}(C_k, D_{k + n}) \] and with differentials \[ \partial_n : [C, D]_n \to [C, D]_{n - 1}, \quad (f_k)_{k \in \ZZ} \mapsto \left(\partial_D \circ f_k - (-1)^n f_{k - 1} \circ \partial_C \right)_{k \in \ZZ} . \]
