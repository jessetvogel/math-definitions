Let \(R\) be \(\ZZ\), \(\QQ\) or \(\RR\). A polarization of an \(R\)-Hodge structure \((H_R, F^\bdot)\) of weight \(k\) is an \(R\)-valued bilinear form \[ Q : H_R \otimes_R H_R \to R \] such that
  • \(Q(v, w) = (-1)^k Q(w, v)\) for all \(v, w \in H_R\),
  • the orthogonal complement of \(F^m\) is \(F^{k - m + 1}\) for all \(m \in \ZZ\),
  • the Hermitian form on \(H_\CC = H_R \otimes \CC\) given by \(v \otimes w \mapsto Q(C u, \overline{v})\), where \(C\) is the Weil operator \(C|_{H^{p, q}} = i^{p - q}\), is positive-definite.
