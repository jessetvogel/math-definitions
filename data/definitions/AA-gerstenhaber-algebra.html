A Gerstenhaber algebra is a graded \(k\)-algebra \(A = \bigoplus_{n \in \ZZ} A^n\), equipped with a bilinear map \([-, -] : A \times A \to A\) of degree \(-1\), such that
  • (graded commutative) \(ab = (-1)^{\deg(a) \deg(b)} ba\) for all homogeneous \(a, b \in A\),
  • (graded skew-symmetric) \([a, b] = -(-1)^{(\deg(a) - 1)(\deg(b) - 1)} [b, a]\) for all homogeneous \(a, b \in A\),
  • (graded Jacobi identity) \([a, [b, c]] = [[a, b], c] + (-1)^{(\deg(a) - 1)(\deg(b) - 1)} [b, [a, c]]\) for all homogeneous \(a, b, c \in A\),
  • (graded Poisson identity) \([a, bc] = [a, b]c + (-1)^{(\deg(a) - 1) \deg(b)} b [a, c]\) for all homogeneous \(a, b, c \in A\).
