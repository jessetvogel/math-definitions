Let \((M, \xi)\) be a contact manifold. The Reeb vector field corresponding to a contact form \(\alpha\) is the vector field \(R_\alpha\) defined by \[ \iota_{R_\alpha} d \alpha = 0 \quad \textup{ and } \quad \iota_{R_\alpha} \alpha = 1 . \] The flow generated by \(R_\alpha\) is called the Reeb flow.
