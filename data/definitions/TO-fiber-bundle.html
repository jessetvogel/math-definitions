A continuous map \(p : E \to B\) is a fiber bundle with fiber \(F\), if for each \(b \in B\) there is an open neighborhood \(U\) and a homeomorphism \(\varphi : p^{-1}(U) \to U \times F\) over \(U\), that is, commutes.
To denote \(p\) is a fiber bundle with fiber \(F\), one often writes \[ F \to B \xrightarrow{p} E . \]
