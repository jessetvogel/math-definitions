A vector bundle \(\mathcal{E}\) over a curve \(C\) is semistable (resp. stable) if \(\mu(\mathcal{F}) \le \mu(\mathcal{E})\) (resp. \(\mu(\mathcal{F}) < \mu(\mathcal{E})\)) for every vector subbundle \(0 \ne \mathcal{F} \subsetneq \mathcal{E}\), where \(\mu(\mathcal{E})\) denotes the slope of \(\mathcal{E}\).
