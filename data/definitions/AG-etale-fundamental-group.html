Let \(X\) be a scheme over a field \(k\), and let \(\overline{x} \to X\) be a geometric point of \(X\). Let \(\textbf{FÉt}/X\) be the category of finite étale coverings \(\pi : Y \to X\) over \(X\). The functor \[ F : \textbf{FÉt}/X \to \textbf{Set}, \quad Y \mapsto \Hom_X(\overline{x}, Y) \] is pro-representable, meaning there exists a direct system \(\tilde{X} = (X_i)_{i \in I}\) in \(\textbf{FÉt}/X\) such that \(F(Y) = \varinjlim_{i \in I} \Hom_X(X_i, Y)\) functorially in \(Y\). Moreover, each \(X_i\) can be chosen Galois over \(X\), so that the order of \(\textup{Aut}_X(X_i)\) equal the degree of \(X_i\) over \(X\).
The étale fundamental group of \(X\) at \(\overline{x}\) is the topological group given by the inverse limit \[ \pi_1^\textup{ét}(X, \overline{x}) = \varprojlim_{i \in I} \textup{Aut}_X(X_i) , \] with the natural topology as an inverse limit of finite discrete groups.
