The category of elements of a functor \(F : \mathcal{C} \to \textbf{Set}\) is the category \(\int_\mathcal{C} F\) whose
  • objects are pairs \((C, x)\) with \(C\) an object of \(\mathcal{C}\) and \(x \in F(C)\),
  • morphisms \((C, x) \to (C', x')\) are morphisms \(f : C \to C'\) with \(F(f)(x) = x'\).
