Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category, and consider the following commutative diagram. Suppose that both rows are exact. The five lemma states that
  • if \(b\) and \(d\) are injective and \(a\) is surjective, then \(c\) is injective.
  • if \(b\) and \(d\) are surjective and \(e\) is injective, then \(c\) is surjective.
  • if \(b\) and \(d\) are isomorphisms, \(a\) is surjective and \(e\) is injective, then \(c\) is an isomorphism.
