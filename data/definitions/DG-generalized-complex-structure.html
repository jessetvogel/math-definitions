Let \(M\) be a smooth manifold. A generalized almost complex structure on \(M\) is an endomorphism \[ \mathcal{J} : TM \oplus T^*M \to TM \oplus T^*M , \] of the generalized tangent bundle on \(M\), such that
  • (complex structure) \(\mathcal{J}^2 = -\id\),
  • (symplectic structure) \(\mathcal{J}^* = -\mathcal{J}\).
A generalized complex structure on \(M\) is a generalized almost complex structure which is integrable, meaning its \(+i\)-eigenbundle \(T_{0, 1} \subset (TM \oplus T^*M)_\CC\) is involutive with respect to the Courant bracket.
