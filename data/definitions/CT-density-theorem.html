Let \(F : \mathcal{C}^\textup{op} \to \textbf{Set}\) be a presheaf on a category \(\mathcal{C}\). The density theorem states that \(F\) is the colimit of the composition \[ \int^\mathcal{C} F \xrightarrow{\pi_\mathcal{C}} \mathcal{C} \xrightarrow{y_{(-)}} \textbf{Set}^{\mathcal{C}^\textup{op}} , \] where \(\int^\mathcal{C} F\) denotes the category of elements of \(F\), and \(y_{(-)}\) the Yoneda embedding.
