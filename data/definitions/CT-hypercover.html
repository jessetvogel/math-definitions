Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a site with finite fiber products. A hypercovering of an object \(X\) in \(\mathcal{C}\) is a simplicial object \(U_\bdot\) in \(\mathcal{C}\) with a covering morphism \(U_0 \to X\) such that, for all \(n \ge 0\), the natural map \[ U_{n + 1} \to (\textbf{cosk}_n(\textbf{sk}_n(U)))_{n + 1} \] is a covering morphism, where \(\textbf{cosk}_n\) and \(\textbf{sk}_n\) denote the simplicial (co)skeleton functors.
