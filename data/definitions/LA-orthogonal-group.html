The orthogonal group of degree \(n\) over a field \(k\), denoted \(\textup{O}(n, k)\), is the group of \(n \times n\) orthogonal matrices with the operation of matrix multiplication.
The special orthogonal group is the subgroup \(\textup{SO}(n, k) \subset \textup{O}(n, k)\) of matrices with determinant \(1\).
