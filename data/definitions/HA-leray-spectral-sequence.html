Let \(X\) and \(Y\) be topological spaces and \(f : X \to Y\) a continuous map. For a sheaf \(\mathcal{F}\) on \(X\), there exists a spectral sequence \[ E_2^{p, q} = H^p(Y, R^q f_* \mathcal{F}) \Rightarrow H^{p + q}(X, \mathcal{F}) \] called the Leray spectral sequence. It is a special case of the Grothendieck spectral sequence.
