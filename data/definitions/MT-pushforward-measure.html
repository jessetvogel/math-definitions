Let \((\Omega, \mathcal{A})\) and \((\Omega', \mathcal{A}')\) be a measurable spaces and \(f : \Omega \to \Omega'\) a measurable function. Given a measure \(\mu\) on \(\mathcal{A}\), the pushforward of \(\mu\) along \(f\) is the measure on \(\Omega'\), denoted \(f_* \mu\), given by \[ f_* \mu (A') = \mu(f^{-1}(A')) \] for all \(A' \in \mathcal{A}'\).
