A norm on a vector space \(V\) over \(\RR\) or \(\CC\) is a function \(\norm{\cdot} : V \to \RR_{\ge 0}\) satisfying
  • (positive definite) \(\norm{x} \ge 0\) for all \(x \in V\) with equality if and only if \(x = 0\),
  • (absolute homogeneity) \(\norm{\lambda x} = |\lambda| x\) for all scalars \(\lambda\) and \(x \in V\),
  • (triangle inequality) \(\norm{x + y} \le \norm{x} + \norm{y}\) for all \(x, y \in V\).
