Let \(k\) be a field with algebraic closure \(\overline{k}\), let \(G\) be a finite group, and let \(V\) be an irreducible representation of \(G\) over \(\overline{k}\) with character \(\chi\). Write \(k(\chi)\) for the field extension of \(k\) obtained by adjoining the values \(\chi(g)\) for all \(g \in G\). The Schur index \(m_k(V)\) of \(V\) is the minimal positive integer \(m\) such that there exists a field extension \(\ell / k(\chi)\) of degree \(m\) and a representation \(W\) of \(G\) over \(\ell\) such that \(V \cong W \otimes_\ell \overline{k}\).
