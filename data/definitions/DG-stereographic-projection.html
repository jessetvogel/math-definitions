Let \(S^n = \{ (x_0, \ldots, x_n) \in \RR^{n + 1} \mid x_0^2 + \cdots x_n^2 = 1 \}\) be the \(n\)-sphere. For any point \(p \in S^n\), stereographic projection is a diffeomorphism \(S^n \setminus \{ p \} \to \RR^n\). For \(p = (1, 0, \ldots, 0)\), it is given by \[ S^n \setminus \{ p \} \to \RR^n, \quad (x_0, \ldots, x_n) \mapsto \left(\frac{x_1}{1 - x_0}, \ldots, \frac{x_n}{1 - x_0}\right) \] with inverse \[ \RR^n \to S^n \setminus \{ p \}, \quad (y_1, \ldots, y_n) \mapsto \left(\frac{y_1^2 + \cdots + y_n^2 - 1}{y_1^2 + \cdots + y_n^2 + 1}, \frac{2 y_1}{y_1^2 + \cdots + y_n^2 + 1}, \ldots, \frac{2 y_n}{y_1^2 + \cdots + y_n^2 + 1}\right) . \]
