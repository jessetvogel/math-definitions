Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category, and \(A\) an object of \(\mathcal{A}\) with a composition series \[ 0 = A_0 \subsetneq A_1 \subsetneq \cdots \subsetneq A_n = A . \] The semisimplification of \(A\) is the direct sum of its composition factors, \[ A^\textup{ss} = \bigoplus_{i = 1}^{n} A_i / A_{i - 1} , \] which is well-defined up to isomorphism by the Jordan–Hölder theorem.
