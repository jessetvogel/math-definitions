Let \(A\) be a unital C*-algebra. A projection over \(A\) is an element \(p \in \textup{Mat}_n(A)\) such that \(p = p^* = p^2\), for some \(n \ge 1\). Two such projections are homotopic if there is a continuous path of projections connecting them. Define \(\textup{K}_0(A)\) to be the free abelian group of homotopy classes of projections over \(A\), modulo the relations
  • \([0_{n \times n}] = 0\) for all \(n \ge 1\),
  • \([p] + [q] = [p \oplus q]\) for all projections \(p\) and \(q\) over \(A\).
The higher K-theory groups are defined by \[ K_n(A) = K_0(S^n A) , \] where \[ SA = \{ f : [0, 1] \to A \;|\; f \textup{ continuous and } f(0) = f(1) = 0 \} \] is the suspension of \(A\).
