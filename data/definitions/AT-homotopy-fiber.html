Let \(f : X \to Y\) be a continuous map of pointed spaces \(X, Y\) with basepoints \(x \in X\) and \(y \in Y\). Then the homotopy fiber of \(f\) is the space \[ \text{hofib}(f) = X \times_Y P Y = \{ (x, \gamma) \in X \times P Y : f(x) = \gamma(1) \} , \] where \(P Y\) denotes the path space of \(Y\).
