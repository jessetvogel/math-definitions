Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category. The \(i\)-th homology object of a chain complex \(A_\bdot\) is the quotient \[ H_i(A_\bdot) = \ker d_i / \im d_{i + 1} . \] Dually, the \(i\)-th cohomology object of a cochain complex \(A^\bdot\) is the quotient \[ H^i(A^\bdot) = \ker d^i / \im d^{i - 1} . \]
