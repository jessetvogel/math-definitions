Let \(M\) be a smooth manifold with a connection \(\nabla\) on the tangent bundle \(TM\) of \(M\). The torsion tensor of \(\nabla\) is the tensor \(T \in \Gamma(TM \otimes \wedge^2 T^* M)\) given by \[ T_\nabla(X, Y) = \nabla_X Y - \nabla_Y X - [X, Y] , \] where \([X, Y]\) denotes the Lie bracket of vector fields.
The connection \(\nabla\) is called torsion-free if \(T_\nabla = 0\).
