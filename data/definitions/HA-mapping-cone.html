Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category, and \(f : A^\bdot \to B^\bdot\) a morphism between cochain complexes in \(\mathcal{A}\). The mapping cone of \(f\) is the complex \(C(f)\) in \(\mathcal{A}\) given by \[ C(f)^i = A^{i + 1} \oplus B^i , \] with differential \[ d_{C(f)}^i = \begin{pmatrix} -d_A^{i + 1} & 0 \\ f^{i + 1} & d_B^i \end{pmatrix} . \]
