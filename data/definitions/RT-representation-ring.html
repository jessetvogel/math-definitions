Given a group \(G\) and a field \(k\), the representation ring \(R_k(G)\) is the free abelian group on isomorphism classes of finite-dimensional \(k\)-representations of \(G\). For the ring structure, addition is given by the direct sum of representations, and multiplication by their tensor product over \(k\).
Equivalently, the representation ring \(R_k(G)\) is the Grothendieck ring of the category of finite-dimensional representations of \(G\).
