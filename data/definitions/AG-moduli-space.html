Let \(F : \textbf{Sch}^\textup{op} \to \textbf{Set}\) be a functor.
A scheme \(M\) is a fine moduli space for \(F\) if \(M\) represents \(F\), that is, there is a natural isomorphism \[ F \simeq \Hom_\textbf{Sch}(-, M) . \]
A scheme \(M\) is a coarse moduli space for \(F\) if there exists a natural transformation \[ \tau : F \Rightarrow \Hom_\textbf{Sch}(-, M) , \] which is universal among all such natural transformations.
