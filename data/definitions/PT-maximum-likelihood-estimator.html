Let \(X_1, \ldots, X_n\) be a random sample from a population \(F_\theta\) depending on some parameter \(\theta\) in a parameter space \(\Theta\). The maximum likelihood estimator (MLE) is the estimator \(\hat{\theta}\) for \(\theta\) given by \[ \hat{\theta}(x) = \operatorname{argmax}_{\theta \in \Theta} L(\theta; x) \] where \(L(\theta; x)\) denotes the likelihood function.
