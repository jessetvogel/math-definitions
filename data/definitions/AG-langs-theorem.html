Let \(G_0\) be an algebraic group over a finite field \(\FF_q\) and let \(G = G_0 \times_{\FF_q} \overline{\FF}_q\). Denote by \(F : G \to G\) the morphism induced by the Frobenius morphism \(F : \overline{\FF}_q \to \overline{\FF}_q, x \mapsto x^q\). The Lang map is the morphism \[ L : G \to G, \quad g \mapsto g^{-1} F(g) . \] Note that the points of \(G\) fixed by \(F\) is given by \(\ker L\).
