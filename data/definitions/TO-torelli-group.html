Let \(X\) be a topological space. There is an action of the mapping class group \(\textup{MCG}(X)\) of \(X\) on the homology of \(X\) since automorphisms of \(X\) isotopic to the identity act trivially on homology. The Torelli group of \(X\) is the subgroup \(\textup{Tor}(X) \subset \textup{MCG}(X)\) which acts trivially on the homology of \(X\).
