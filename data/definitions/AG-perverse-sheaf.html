Let \(X\) be a variety over a field \(k\), and denote by \(\textbf{D}^b_c(X, F)\) the full subcategory of the derived category \(\textbf{D}^b(X, F)\) of sheaves of \(F\)-vector spaces on \(X\), for some field \(F\), of complexes whose cohomology is constructible.
A \(t\)-structure on \(\textbf{D}^b_c(X, F)\) is given by \[ \begin{aligned} D^{\le 0} &= \{ K \in \textbf{D}^b_c(X, F) \mid \dim \operatorname{supp} H^i(K) \le -i \textup{ for all } i \in \ZZ \} \\ D^{\ge 0} &= \{ K \in \textbf{D}^b_c(X, F) \mid K^\vee \in D^{\le 0} \} \end{aligned} \] where \(K^\vee = R\iHom(K, \pi^! F)\) denotes the dual of \(K\), with \(\pi : X \to \Spec k\). The heart of this \(t\)-structure \[ \operatorname{Perv}(X, F) = D^\heartsuit = D^{\le 0} \cap D^{\ge 0} \] is the abelian category of perverse sheaves.
