Let \(M\) be a smooth manifold. The generalized tangent bundle of \(M\) is the direct sum \(TM \oplus T^*M\) of the tangent bundle \(TM\) and the cotangent bundle \(T^*M\).
There is a natural symmetric bilinear form on the generalized tangent bundle, given by \[ \langle X + \xi, Y + \eta \rangle = \xi(Y) + \eta(X) , \] for \(X + \xi, Y + \eta \in \Gamma(TM \oplus T^*M)\).
