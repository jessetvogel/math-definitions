Let \(M\) be a complex manifold. The Riemannâ€“Hilbert correspondence states there is an equivalence of categories \[ \textbf{LS}_\CC(M) \simeq \textbf{Vect}_\CC^\nabla(M) , \] between the category of local systems of \(\CC\)-vector spaces on \(M\), and the category of complex vector bundles with a flat connection on \(M\).
Explicitly, to a local system \(\mathcal{L}\) is assigned the vector bundle (with sheaf of sections) \(\mathcal{L} \otimes_\CC \mathcal{O}_M\) with connection induced by the exterior derivative \(d : \mathcal{O}_M \to \Omega_M^1\). Conversely, to a vector bundle \(\mathcal{E}\) with flat connection \(\nabla\) is assigned the local system given by the sheaf of horizontal sections \(\ker(\nabla) \subset \mathcal{E}\).
