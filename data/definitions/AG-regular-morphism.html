Let \(f : X \to Y\) be a morphism of schemes, and assume that all fibers \(X_y\) are locally noetherian. Then \(f\) regular at \(x \in X\) if \(f\) is flat at \(x\) and the fiber \(X_{f(x)}\) is geometrically regular at \(x\) over \(k(y)\). The morphism \(f\) is regular if it is regular at all points \(x \in X\).
