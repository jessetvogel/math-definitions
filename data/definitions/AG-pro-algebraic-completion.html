Let \(\Gamma\) be a group and \(k\) be a field. Consider the collection of pairs \((G, \varphi_G)\) consisting of an affine group scheme \(G\) over \(k\) and a group morphism \(\varphi_G : \Gamma \to G(k)\). Define a partial order on this collection by setting \((G, \varphi_G) \le (H, \varphi_H)\) if there exists a morphism \(f : G \to H\) such that \(\varphi_H = f \circ \varphi_G\). The pro-algebraic completion of \(\Gamma\) is the inverse limit \[ \widehat{\Gamma}^\textup{alg} = \varprojlim_{(G, \varphi_G)} G . \]
