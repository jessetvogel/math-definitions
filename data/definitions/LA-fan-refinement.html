Let \(\Delta\) be a fan in \(\RR^n\). A refinement of \(\Delta\) is a fan \(\Delta'\) in \(\RR^n\) such that
  • for every \(\sigma' \in \Delta'\), there exists some \(\sigma \in \Delta\) with \(\sigma' \subset \sigma\),
  • \(|\Delta| = |\Delta'|\), where \(|\Delta| = \bigcup_{\sigma \in \Delta} \sigma\).
