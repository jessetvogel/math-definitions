Explicitly, it is the probability distribution on \(\RR\) with PDF \[ f(x) = \frac{\Gamma((\nu + 1) / 2)}{\sqrt{\pi \nu} \Gamma(\nu / 2)} (1 + x^2 / \nu)^{-(\nu + 1) / 2} \] for \(x \in \RR\), where \(\nu = n - 1\) and \(\Gamma\) denotes the gamma function.
