Let \(X\) be a smooth projective scheme of dimension \(n\), and let \(\omega_X\) be its canonical sheaf. Then Serre duality states that for every coherent sheaf \(\mathcal{F}\) on \(X\) there is a natural isomorphism \[ H^i(X, \mathcal{F}^\vee \otimes \omega_X) \simeq H^{n - i}(X, \mathcal{F})^\vee . \]
