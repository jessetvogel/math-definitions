A lattice is a partially ordered set \((P, \le)\) such that
  • (least upper bound) any two elements \(x, y \in P\) have a least upper bound \(x \vee y\),
  • (greatest lower bound) any two elements \(x, y \in P\) have a greatest lower bound \(x \wedge y\),
  • (least element) there exists a least element \(0 \in P\),
  • (greatest element) there exists a greatest element \(1 \in P\).
