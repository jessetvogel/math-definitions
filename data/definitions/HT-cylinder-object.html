Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a model category, and \(X\) an object of \(\mathcal{C}\). A cylinder object for \(X\) is an object \(\textup{Cyl}(X)\) of \(\mathcal{C}\) together with a factorization \[ X \coprod X \to \textup{Cyl}(X) \xrightarrow{\sim} X , \] of the natural map \(X \coprod X \to X\), where the second arrow is a weak equivalence.
