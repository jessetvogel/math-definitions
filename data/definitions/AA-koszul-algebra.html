A Koszul algebra \(A\) over a field \(k\) is a graded \(k\)-algebra, such that the ground field \(k\) has a linear minimal graded free resolution, that is, there exists an exact sequence \[ \cdots \to A(-i)^{b_i} \to \cdots \to A(-2)^{b_2} \to A(-1)^{b_1} \to A \to k \to 0 , \] for some \(b_i \ge 0\).
