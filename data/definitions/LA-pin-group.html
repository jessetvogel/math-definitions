The pin group \(\text{Pin}(V, q)\) of a vector space \(V\) over \(k\) with a quadratic form \(q : V \to k\) is the subgroup of the units of the Clifford algebra \(\text{Cl}(V, q)\) of elements of the form \(v_1 v_2 \cdots v_k\) with \(q(v_i) = 1\).
As a special case, \(\text{Pin}(n) = \text{Pin}(\RR^n, \langle \cdot, \cdot \rangle)\).
