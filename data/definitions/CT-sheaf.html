A presheaf \(\mathcal{F} : \mathcal{C}^\textup{op} \to \textbf{Set}\) on a site \(\mathcal{C}\) is a sheaf if for every covering sieve \(\{ U_i \to U \}_{i \in I}\), the diagram \[ \mathcal{F}(U) \to \prod_{i \in I} \mathcal{F}(U_i) \rightrightarrows \prod_{i, j \in I} \mathcal{F}(U_i \times_U U_j) \] is an equalizer.
