Let \(k\) be a field, and let \(\mathcal{A}\) be a \(k\)-linear category. Then \(\mathcal{A}\) is locally finite if
  • the vector space \(\Hom_\mathcal{A}(A, B)\) is finite-dimensional for all objects \(A\) and \(B\) of \(\mathcal{A}\),
  • every object of \(\mathcal{A}\) has finite length.
If moreover
  • \(\mathcal{A}\) contains finitely many simple objects (up to isomorphism),
then \(\mathcal{A}\) is finite.
