A functor \(F : \mathcal{C} \to \textbf{Set}\) is representable if it is naturally isomorphic to \(\Hom_\mathcal{C}(X, -)\) for some object \(X\) of \(\mathcal{C}\).
Similarly, a functor \(F : \mathcal{C}^\textup{op} \to \textbf{Set}\) (i.e. a presheaf) is representable if it is naturally isomorphic to \(\Hom_\mathcal{C}(-, X)\) for some object \(X\) of \(\mathcal{C}\).
