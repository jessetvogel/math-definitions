Let \(M\) be an \(n\)-dimensional smooth manifold. The tangent bundle of \(M\), denoted \(TM\), is defined as the disjoint union of the tangent spaces at all points of \(M\), \[ TM = \bigsqcup_{p \in M} T_p M . \] It naturally has the structure of a \(2n\)-dimensional smooth manifold such that the projection map \[ \pi : TM \to M, \quad (p, v) \mapsto p \] is smooth.
