Let \(A\) be a commutative C*-algebra. The Gelfand spectrum of \(A\) is the set of characters \[ \textup{sp}(A) = \{ \phi : A \to \CC \;|\; \phi \ne 0 \textup{ is an algebra morphism} \} , \] with the weak *-topology: the coarsest topology such that for all \(a \in A\) the functions \(\hat{a} : \textup{sp}(A) \to \CC\) given by \(\hat{a}(\chi) = \chi(a)\) are continuous.
It can be shown that \(\textup{sp}(A)\) is compact and Hausdorff.
