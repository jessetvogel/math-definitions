Let \(M\) be a smooth manifold with a connection \(\nabla\) on the tangent bundle \(TM\), and \(p \in M\) a point. Any tangent vector \(v \in T_p M\) induces a geodesic \(\gamma : [0, 1] \to M\) starting at \(\gamma(0) = p\) with velocity \(\gamma'(0) = v\). The exponential map at \(p\) is the map \[ \exp : T_p M \to M, \quad v \mapsto \gamma(1) . \]
