If \(G\) is an algebraic group acting on a scheme \(S\), the quotient stack \([S/G]\) is defined as the category over \(\textbf{Sch}\) whose objects over \(T\) are principal \(G\)-bundles \(P \to T\), with a \(G\)-equivariant morphism \(P \to S\). Its morphisms are \(G\)-equivariant isomorphisms which are compatible with the morphisms to \(S\).
