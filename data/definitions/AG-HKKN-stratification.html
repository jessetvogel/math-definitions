Let \(G\) be a reductive algebraic group over a field \(k\) acting on a variety \(X\). For any one-parameter subgroup \(\lambda : \GG_m \to G\), let \[ P_\lambda = \left\{ g \in G \mid \lim_{t \to 0} \lambda(t) \cdot g \cdot \lambda(t)^{-1} \textup{ exists} \right\} \] and write \(X^\lambda\) for the fixed-point locus of \(X\). For any connected component \(Z_0^\lambda\) of \(X^\lambda\), let \[ Z_\lambda = \left\{ x \in X \mid \lim_{t \to 0} \lambda(t) \cdot x \in Z_\lambda^0 \right\} \quad \textup{ and } \quad S_\lambda = G \cdot Z_\lambda . \] An HKKN stratification of \(X\) is a sequence of open subvarieties \[ X = X_0 \supset X_1 \supset X_1 \supset \cdots \supset X_n \] together with one-parameter subgroups \(\lambda_i : \GG_m \to G\) and choices of connected components \(Z_{\lambda_i}^0 \subset X^{\lambda_i}\) for \(1 \le i \le n\), such that
  • \(X_i = X_{i - 1} \setminus S_{\lambda_i}\),
  • the morphism \(G \times_{P_{\lambda_i}} Z_{\lambda_i} \to S_{\lambda_i}\) given by \((g, z) \mapsto g \cdot z\) is an isomorphism,
  • \(S_{\lambda_i}\) is closed in \(X_{i - 1}\),
for all \(1 \le i \le n\).
