Let \((M, g)\) be an \(n\)-dimensional orientable Riemannian manifold. A spin structure on \(M\) is a principal \(\text{Spin}(n)\)-bundle \(P \to M\) together with a bundle morphism \(f : P \to F_{\text{SO}(n)}(M)\) to the oriented orthonormal tangent frame bundle, equivariant with respect to the double cover \(\text{Spin}(n) \to \text{SO}(n)\).
