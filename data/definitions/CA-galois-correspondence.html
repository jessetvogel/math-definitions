Let \(\ell/k\) be a Galois extension with Galois group \(G = \text{Gal}(\ell/k)\). The Galois correspondence states there is an inclusion-reversing bijection between the set of intermediate fields \(k \subset m \subset \ell\) and the set of subgroups of \(G\). Moreover, a subgroup \(H \subset G\) is normal if and only if the intermediate field \(m = \ell^H\) is a normal extension of \(k\). In this case, \(m/k\) is a Galois extension with Galois group \(G/H\).
