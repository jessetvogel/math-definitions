Let \(f : R \to S\) be morphism of commutative rings. Restriction of scalars along \(f\) is the functor \[ U : \textbf{Mod}_S \to \textbf{Mod}_R \] assigning to an \(S\)-module \(N\) the \(R\)-module with underlying abelian group \(N\) and whose \(R\)-module structure is given by \(r \cdot n = f(r) \cdot n\) for all \(r \in R\) and \(n \in N\).
