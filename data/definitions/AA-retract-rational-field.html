Let \(k\) be a field. A field extension \(K/k\) is retract rational if there exists a \(k\)-algebra \(A\) in \(K\), whose field of fractions is \(K\), a non-zero polynomial \(f \in k[x_1, \ldots, x_n]\), and \(k\)-algebra morphisms \(\varphi : A \to k[x_1, \ldots, x_n, f^{-1}]\) and \(\psi : k[x_1, \ldots, x_n, f^{-1}]\) such that \(\psi \circ \phi = \id_A\).
