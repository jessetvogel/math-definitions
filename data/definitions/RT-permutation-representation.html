Let \(G\) be a group with an action on a finite set \(X\). The permutation representation of the action over a field \(k\) is the representation of \(G\), \[ \rho : G \to \textup{GL}\left( \bigoplus_{x \in X} k \right), \quad \rho(g)\left( (a_x)_{x \in X} \right) = \left( a_{g^{-1} \cdot x} \right)_{x \in X} . \]
