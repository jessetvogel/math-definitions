Let \(A = (a_{ij})\) be an \(n \times n\) generalized Cartan matrix. The Kacâ€“Moody algebra of \(A\) is the Lie algebra \(\mathfrak{g}(A)\) over \(\CC\), defined by generators \(H_i, X_i, Y_i\) for \(i = 1, \ldots, n\) and relations \[ [H_i, H_j] = 0, \quad [H_i, X_j] = a_{ij} X_j, \quad [H_i, Y_j] = -a_{ij} Y_j, \quad [X_i, Y_j] = \delta_{ij} H_i , \textup{ for all } i, j \] \[ \textup{ and } \left(\textup{ad}_{X_i}\right)^{1 - a_{ij}}(X_j) = 0, \quad \left(\textup{ad}_{Y_i}\right)^{1 - a_{ij}}(Y_j) = 0, \textup{ for } i \ne j , \] where \(\textup{ad}_X(Y) = [X, Y]\) denotes the adjoint representation.
