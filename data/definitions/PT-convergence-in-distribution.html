Let \((\Omega, \mathcal{A}, \PP)\) be a probability space. A sequence \(X_1, X_2, \ldots : \Omega \to \RR\) of random variables converges in distribution to a random variable \(X : \Omega \to \RR\) if \[ \lim_{n \to \infty} F_{X_n}(x) = F_X(x) \] for every \(x \in \RR\) at which \(F_X\), the CDF of \(X\), is continuous.
