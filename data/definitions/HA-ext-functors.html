Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category and \(A\) an object of \(\mathcal{A}\). The functor \[ \Hom_{\mathcal{A}}(A, -) : \mathcal{A} \to \textbf{Ab} \] is left exact, so (if \(\mathcal{A}\) has enough injectives) it has right derived functors, which are known as the Ext functors \[ \text{Ext}^i_\mathcal{A}(A, B) = \text{R}^i \Hom_\mathcal{A}(A, B) . \]
