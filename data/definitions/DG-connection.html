Let \(\pi : E \to M\) be a vector bundle over a smooth manifold \(M\). A connetion on \(E\) is a linear differential operator \[ \nabla : \Gamma(E) \to \Gamma(T^*M \otimes E) \] satisfying the Leibniz rule \[ \nabla(fs) = df \otimes s + f \nabla s \] for all smooth functions \(f\) and sections \(s \in \Gamma(E)\).
