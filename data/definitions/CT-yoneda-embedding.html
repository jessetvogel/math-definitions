Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category. The Yoneda embedding is the functor \[ y_{(-)} : \mathcal{C} \to \textbf{Set}^{\mathcal{C}^\text{op}} \] which assigns to an object \(X\) of \(\mathcal{C}\) the functor \[ y_X = \Hom_{\mathcal{C}}(-, X) , \] and to a morphism \(f : X \to Y\) the natural transformation \[ y_f : y_X \Rightarrow y_Y , \] given by \((y_f)_Z(g) = f \circ g\) for any object \(Z\) and morphism \(g : Z \to X\) in \(\mathcal{C}\).
