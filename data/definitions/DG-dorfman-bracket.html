Let \(M\) be a smooth manifold. The Dorfman bracket is the bracket \([\cdot, \cdot]\) on the generalized tangent bundle given by \[ [X + \xi, Y + \eta] = [X, Y] + \mathcal{L}_X \eta - \mathcal{L}_Y \xi + d \iota_Y \xi , \] for \(X + \xi, Y + \eta \in \Gamma(TM \oplus T^*M)\), where \([X, Y]\) denotes the Lie bracket of vector fields, and \(\mathcal{L}\) the Lie derivative.
