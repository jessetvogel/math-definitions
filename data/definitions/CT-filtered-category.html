A category \(\mathcal{C}\) is filtered if
  • it is non-empty,
  • for every two objects \(x, y\) in \(\mathcal{C}\), there exists an object \(z\) and arrows \(x \to z\) and \(y \to z\),
  • for every two morphisms \(f, g : x \to y\) in \(\mathcal{C}\), there exists an object \(z\) and an arrow \(h : x \to y\) with \(hf = hg\).
Reversing arrows gives the definition of a cofiltered category.
