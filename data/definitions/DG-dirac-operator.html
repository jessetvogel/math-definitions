Let \((M, g)\) be a Riemannian manifold and \(E \to M\) be a complex vector bundle. A Dirac operator on \(E\) is a first order linear differential operator \(D : \Gamma(E) \to \Gamma(E)\) on \(E\) such that \[ \sigma_D(\xi)^2 = - g(\xi, \xi) \id_E \] for all \(\xi \in T^*M\), where \(\sigma_D : T^*M \to \operatorname{End}(E)\) denotes the principal symbol of \(D\) given by \(\sigma_D(df) = [D, f]\) for all \(f \in C^\infty(M)\).
