Let \(X\) be a connected non-singular variety over \(\CC\). The Riemann existence theorem states there is an equivalence of categories \[ \textbf{FÉt}/X \simeq \textbf{FCov}(X(\CC)), \quad (\pi : Y \to X) \mapsto (\pi : Y(\CC) \to X(\CC)) \] between the category of finite étale coverings over \(X\) and the category of finite covering spaces of the analytification \(X(\CC)\).
