Let \(M\) be a smooth manifold. The interior product is the contraction of a differential form with a vector field. That is, if \(X\) is a vector field on \(M\), then \[ \iota_X : \Omega^k(M) \to \Omega^{k - 1} \] is the map defined by \[ (\iota_X \omega)(Y_1, \ldots, Y_{k - 1}) = \omega(X, Y_1, \ldots, Y_{k - 1}) \] for any vector fields \(Y_1, \ldots, Y_{k - 1}\).
