A dinatural transformation between two functors \(F, G : \mathcal{C}^\textup{op} \times \mathcal{C} \to \mathcal{D}\), denoted \(\alpha : F \xrightarrow{\cdot\cdot} G\) is a collection of morphisms \(\alpha_X : F(X, X) \to G(X, X)\), such that for every morphism \(f : X \to Y\) the diagram commutes.
