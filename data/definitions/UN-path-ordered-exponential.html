Let \(A\) be a real or complex algebra and \(a : \RR \to A\) a continuous function. The path-ordered exponential of \(a\) is the function \(\textup{P} \exp [a](t) : \RR \to A\) given by \[ \textup{P} \exp [a] (t) = \sum_{n = 0}^\infty \int_0^t \int_0^{t'_n} \int_0^{t'_{n - 1}} \cdots \int_0^{t'_2} a(t'_n) \cdots a(t'_1) d t'_1 \cdots d t'_{n - 2} d t'_{n - 1} d t'_n . \]
