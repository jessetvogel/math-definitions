A Lie groupoid is a groupoid \(X_1 \rightrightarrows X_0\), whose sets of objects \(X_0\) and arrows \(X_1\) are smooth manifolds, such that
  • inversion \((-)^{-1} : X_1 \to X_1\), composition \(\circ : X_1 \times_{X_0} X_1 \to X_1\) and the identity map \(\id_{(-)} : X_0 \to X_1\), are all smooth,
  • the source and target maps \(s, t : X_1 \to X_0\) are smooth surjective submersions.
