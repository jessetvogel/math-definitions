Let \(M\) be a smooth manifold. A compatible triple on \(M\) is a triple \((\omega, J, g)\), where \(\omega\) is a symplectic form on \(M\), \(J\) is an almost complex structure on \(M\), and \(g\) is a Riemannian metric on \(M\), such that \[ g(X, Y) = \omega(X, J(Y)) \] for all vector fields \(X\) and \(Y\) on \(M\).
