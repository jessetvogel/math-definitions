Let \(f : X \to Y\) be a continuous map of pointed spaces \(X, Y\) with basepoints \(x \in X\) and \(y \in Y\). Then the homotopy cofiber of \(f\) is the space \[ C(f) = Y \cup_X CX , \] where \(CX = X \times [0, 1] / (X \times \{ 1 \} \cup \{ x \} \times [0, 1])\) denotes the reduced cone of \(X\), and \(X\) is identified with \(X \times \{ 0 \}\).
