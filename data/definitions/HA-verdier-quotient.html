Let \(\mathcal{T}\) be a triangulated category and \(\mathcal{S} \subset \mathcal{T}\) a triangulated subcategory. The Verdier quotient \(\mathcal{T}/\mathcal{S}\) is a triangulated category equipped with an additive and exact quotient functor \[ Q : \mathcal{T} \to \mathcal{T}/\mathcal{S} , \] which is universal among all functors \(\mathcal{T} \to \mathcal{D}\) which send objects of \(\mathcal{S}\) to objects isomorphic to zero.
