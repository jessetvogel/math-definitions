A knot is an embedding \(K : S^1 \to \RR^3\) from the circle \(S^1\) to \(\RR^3\).
Two knots \(K_1\) and \(K_2\) are equivalent if there exists a smooth map \[ H : [0, 1] \times \RR^3 \to \RR^3 \] such that \(H(0, -) = \id_{\RR^3}\), \(H(1, -) \circ K_1 = K_2\) and \(H(t, -)\) is a diffeomorphism for all \(t \in [0, 1]\).
