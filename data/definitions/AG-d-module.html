Let \(X\) be a smooth scheme over an algebraically closed field \(k\) of characteristic zero. Let \(\mathcal{D}_X\) be the sheaf of differential operators on \(X\), that is, the \(\mathcal{O}_X\)-algebra such that for any open \(U \subset X\), \(\mathcal{D}_X(U) \subset \textup{End}_k(\mathcal{O}_X(U))\) is the subalgebra generated by multiplication by functions \(\mathcal{O}_X(U)\) and derivations \(\mathcal{T}_X(U)\).
A \(\mathcal{D}\)-module on \(X\) is a \(\mathcal{D}_X\)-module on \(X\).
