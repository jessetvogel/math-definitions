Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category, and write \(y_X = \Hom_{\mathcal{C}}(-, X)\). The Yoneda lemma states that for every \(F : \mathcal{C}^\text{op} \to \text{Set}\) and \(X\) in \(\mathcal{C}\) there is a bijection \[ \Hom(y_X, F) \xrightarrow{\sim} F(X) \] natural in \(X\) and \(F\). As a consequence, the Yoneda embedding \[ y_{(-)} : \mathcal{C} \to \text{Set}^{\mathcal{C}^\text{op}} \] is full and faithful.
