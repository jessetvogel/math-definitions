Let \(X\) be a topological space, \(X' \subset X\) a subspace, and \(A\) an abelian group. The relative chain complex of \((X, X')\) is defined as the quotient complex \[ C_n(X, X'; A) = C_n(X; A) / C_n(X'; A) . \] Its homology groups \[ H_n(X, X'; A) = H_n(C_\bullet(X, X; A)) \] are called relative homology groups of \((X, X')\).
