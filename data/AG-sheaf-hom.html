Let \(\mathcal{F}\) and \(\mathcal{G}\) be sheaves of abelian groups on a topological space \(X\). The sheaf hom of \(\mathcal{F}\) and \(\mathcal{G}\) is the sheaf \(\underline{\Hom}(\mathcal{F}, \mathcal{G})\) given by \[ \underline{\Hom}(\mathcal{F}, \mathcal{G})(U) = \Hom(\mathcal{F}|_U, \mathcal{G}|_U) . \]
