Writing \[ F = \sum_{n = 0}^{\infty} a_n t^n = a_0 + a_1 t + a_2 t^2 + \cdots , \] we obtain the coefficients \(a_n\) from the \(n\)-th derivatives of \(F\) as \[ a_n = \frac{F^{(n)}(0)}{n!} . \]
