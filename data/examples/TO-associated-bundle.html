Let \(M\) be an \(n\)-dimensional smooth manifold, and let \(F(TM)\) be the frame bundle of \(M\), which is a principal \(\textup{GL}_n(\RR)\)-bundle. Given a representation \(\rho : \textup{GL}_n(\RR) \to \textup{GL}(V)\), we can form the associated (vector) bundle \[ F(TM) \times_\rho V \to M . \]
