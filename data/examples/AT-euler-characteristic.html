• Since \(H_i(S^n; \QQ)\) is non-trivial only for \(i = 0\) and \(i = n\), we find \[ \chi(S^n) = \dim_\QQ H_0(S^n; \QQ) + (-1)^n \dim_\QQ H_n(S^n; \QQ) = 1 + (-1)^n = \left\{ \begin{array}{cl} 0 & \textup{ if $n$ odd}, \\ 2 & \textup{ if $n$ even}. \end{array} \right. \]
