There is an adjunction between \(T\text{-Alg}\) and \(\mathcal{C}\) yielding the initial monad. The forgetful functor \(U : T\text{-Alg} \to \mathcal{C}\), mapping \((X, h)\) to \(h\), has a left adjoint \(F : \mathcal{C} \to T\text{-Alg}\), mapping an object \(X\) to the \(T\)-algebra \((TX, \mu_X)\), and a morphism \(f : X \to Y\) to \(Tf\). One can show \(F \dashv U\) and clearly \(T = UF\).
