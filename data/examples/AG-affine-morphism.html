• Any morphism \(f : \Spec(R) \to \Spec(S)\) between affine schemes is affine.
  • The inclusion \(\iota : \AA^2_k \setminus \{ (0, 0) \} \to \AA^2_k\) is not affine, since \(\AA^2_k \setminus \{ (0, 0) \}\) is not affine.
  • Let \(Y_1 = Y_2 = \AA^2_k\), and let \(Y\) be obtained by gluing \(Y_1\) and \(Y_2\) along \(\AA^2_k \setminus \{ (0, 0) \}\). Then the inclusions \(\iota_i : Y_i \to Y\) are not affine, since \(j_1^{-1}(Y_2) = j_2^{-1}(Y_1) = \AA^2_k \setminus \{ (0, 0) \}\) is not affine.
