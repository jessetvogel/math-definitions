Given an exceptional sequence \(E_1, \ldots, E_n\) in \(\mathcal{D}\), the subcategories \[ \mathcal{D}_1 = \langle E_1 \rangle, \ldots, \mathcal{D}_n = \langle E_n \rangle , \] form a semi-orthogonal sequence, and if the \(E_i\) generate \(\mathcal{D}\), then they form a semi-orthogonal decomposition.
The derived category \(\textbf{D}^\textup{b}(\PP^n)\) of coherent sheaves on projective space has a semi-orthogonal decomposition \[ \textbf{D}^\textup{b}(\PP^n) = \langle \mathcal{O}(-n), \mathcal{O}(-n + 1), \ldots, \mathcal{O}(-1), \mathcal{O} \rangle . \]
