Note that an ordinary limit of a functor \(F : \mathcal{I} \to \mathcal{C}\), if it exists, can be computed via the Yoneda embedding on the level of presheaves: \[ \begin{aligned} \Hom_\mathcal{C}(C, \lim F) &\isom \lim (\Hom_\mathcal{C}(C, F(-))) \\ &\isom \Hom_\textbf{Set}(\textup{pt}, \lim (\Hom_\mathcal{C}(C, F(-)))) \\ &\isom \Hom_{\textbf{Set}^\mathcal{I}}(\Delta_{\textup{pt}}, \Hom_\mathcal{C}(C, F(-))) . \end{aligned} \] In particular, the weighted limit reduces to the ordinary limit when \(W : \mathcal{I} \to \textbf{Set}\) is the constant functor \(\Delta_\textup{pt}\).
