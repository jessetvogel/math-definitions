• The symmetric group \(S_n\) is complete for \(n \ne 2, 6\). For \(n = 2\), the group \(S_2 \isom \ZZ/2\ZZ\) has non-trivial center, and for \(n = 6\), the group \(S_6\) has an outer automorphism determined by \[ (1 \; 2) \mapsto (1 \; 2)(3 \; 4) (5 \; 6), \qquad (1 \; 2 \; 3 \; 4 \; 5 \; 6) \mapsto (2 \; 4) (3 \; 6 \; 5) . \]
