For any Lie algebra \(\mathfrak{g}\), there is the adjoint representation \[ \textup{ad}_\mathfrak{g} : \mathfrak{g} \to \mathfrak{gl}(\mathfrak{g}), \quad x \mapsto \textup{ad}_x = [x, -] . \] Indeed, this is linear map respects the Lie bracket by the Jacobi identity, \[ [[x, y], z] = [x, [y, z]] - [y, [x, z]] . \]
