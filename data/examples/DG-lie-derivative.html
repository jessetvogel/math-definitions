When \(f\) is a smooth function, we have \[ \begin{aligned} (\mathcal{L}_X f)(p) &= \frac{d}{dt}\Big|_{t = 0} \left(\left(\varphi^t_X\right)^* f \right)(p) \\ &= \frac{d}{dt}\Big|_{t = 0} f \left(\varphi^t_X(p)\right) \\ &= df_p \left( \frac{d}{dt}\Big|_{t = 0} \varphi^t_X(p) \right) \\ &= df_p(X_p) \\ &= X(f)(p) . \end{aligned} \]
