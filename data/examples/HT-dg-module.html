Any object \(x\) of a dg category \(\mathcal{A}\) produces the right dg module \[ \widehat{x} = \Hom_\mathcal{A}(-, x) . \] A dg module of this form is called representable. This construction gives the Yoneda dg functor \[ \widehat{(-)} : \mathcal{A} \to \mathcal{M}\textbf{od}_\mathcal{A}, \quad x \mapsto \widehat{x} , \] which is full and faithful.
