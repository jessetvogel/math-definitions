• The sample mean is the statistic given by the average of the values in a random sample, that is, \[ \overline{X} = \frac{X_1 + \cdots + X_n}{n} . \]
  • The sample variance is the statistic given by \[ S^2 = \frac{1}{n - 1} \sum_{i = 1}^{n} (X_i - \overline{X})^2 \] and the sample standard deviation is the statistic given by \(S = \sqrt{S^2}\).
