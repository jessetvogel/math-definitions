Let \(\ell/k\) be a finite field extension of degree \(s\). Given an affine variety \(X = \Spec(\ell[x_1, \ldots, x_n]) / (f_1, \ldots, f_m)\) over \(\ell\), the Weil restriction \(\textup{Res}_{\ell/k}(X)\) is given by \(\Spec(k[y_{1, 1}, \ldots, y_{n, s}]) / (g_{1, 1}, \ldots, g_{m, s})\), where \(y_{i, j}\) and \(g_{i, j}\) are given by \(x_i = y_{i, 1} e_1 + \ldots + y_{i, s} e_s\) and \(f_i = g_{i, 1} e_1 + \ldots + g_{i, s} e_s\), for a \(k\)-basis \(e_1, \ldots, e_s\) of \(\ell\).
In particular,
  • \(\textup{Res}_{\ell/k}(\Spec(\ell)) = \Spec(k)\)
  • \(\textup{Res}_{\ell/k}(\AA^n_\ell) = \AA^{ns}_k\).
