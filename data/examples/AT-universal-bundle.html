• For \(G = \{ 1 \}\), the map \(\star \to \star\) is a universal \(G\)-bundle.
  • For \(G = \CC^*\), the map \(\CC^\infty \setminus \{ 0 \} \to \CC \PP^\infty\) is a universal \(G\)-bundle.
  • For \(G = S^1\), the map \(S^\infty \to \CC \PP^\infty\) is a universal \(G\)-bundle.
  • For \(G = \textup{GL}_n(\CC)\), the map \[ M \to \textup{Gr}(n, \CC^\infty) \] is a universal \(G\)-bundle, where \(\textup{Gr}(n, \CC^\infty)\) denotes the Grassmanian of \(n\)-dimensional subspaces in \(\CC^\infty\), and \(M\) denotes the space of \(\infty \times n\) matrices of full rank \(n\).
