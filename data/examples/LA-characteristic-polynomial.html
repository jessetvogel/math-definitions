The coefficients of the characteristic polynomial are given by \[ p_A(\lambda) = \sum_{k = 0}^{n} \lambda^{n - k} (-1)^k \textup{tr}(\wedge^k A) , \] where \(\wedge^k A : \wedge^k k^n \to \wedge^k k^n\) is the \(k\)-th exterior power of \(A\). In particular, the constant coefficient is the determinant of \(A\).
