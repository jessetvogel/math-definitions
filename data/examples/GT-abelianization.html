Let \(G = \textup{GL}_n(\RR)\) be the the general linear group. The determinant \(\det : G \to \RR^*\) has constant value \(1\) on \([G, G]\), since the determinant of products of commutators is always \(1\). Conversely, after showing that any matrix of determinant \(1\) can be written as a product of commutators, it follows that \[ \det : G / [G, G] \xrightarrow{\sim} \RR^* \] is an isomorphism.
