Consider the three points \((2, 1), (3, 5)\) and \((5, 19)\). The Lagrange basis polynomials are \[ \begin{aligned} \ell_0(x) &= \frac{(x - 3)(x - 5)}{(2 - 3)(2 - 5)} = \tfrac{1}{3} (x - 5)(x - 3) , \\ \ell_1(x) &= \frac{(x - 2)(x - 5)}{(3 - 2)(3 - 5)} = -\tfrac{1}{2} (x - 5)(x - 2) , \\ \ell_2(x) &= \frac{(x - 2)(x - 3)}{(5 - 2)(5 - 3)} = \tfrac{1}{6} (x - 3)(x - 2) . \end{aligned} \] Hence, the Lagrange interpolation polynomial is \[ L(x) = \ell_0(x) + 5 \ell_1(x) + 19 \ell_2(x) = x^2 - x - 1 . \]
