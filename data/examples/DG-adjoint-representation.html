The Jacobi identity for a Lie algebra \(\mathfrak{g}\) is equivalent to saying \(\textup{ad}_X\) is a derivation for all \(X \in \mathfrak{g}\), that is, \[ \textup{ad}_X([Y, Z]) = [\textup{ad}_X(Y), Z] + [Y, \textup{ad}_X(Z)] . \] Moreover, from the Jacobi identity follows that \[ \textup{ad}_{[X, Y]}(Z) = [[X, Y], Z] = [X, [Y, Z]] - [Y, [X, Z]] = [\textup{ad}_X, \textup{ad}_Y](Z) , \] which shows that the map \(\textup{ad} : \mathfrak{g} \to \mathfrak{gl}(\mathfrak{g})\) is a Lie algebra homomorphism.
