Geometric realization is left adjoint to the singular functor \(\textup{Sing} : \textbf{Top} \to \textbf{Set}_\Delta\). Indeed, for any simplicial set \(X\) and topological space \(S\), we have a natural bijection \[ \begin{aligned} \Hom(\colim_{\Delta^n \to X} |\Delta^n|, S) &= \lim_{\Delta^n \to X} \Hom(|\Delta^n|, S) = \lim_{\Delta^n \to X} \Hom(\Delta^n, \textup{Sing}(S)) \\ & = \Hom(\colim_{\Delta^n \to X} \Delta^n, \textup{Sing}(S)) = \Hom(X, \textup{Sing}(S)) . \end{aligned} \]
