Let \(f : X \to Y\) be a morphism in \(E\). Let us prove the projection formula: \[ f_! A \otimes B \cong f_! (A \otimes f^* B) \] in \(D(Y)\) for any \(A \in D(X)\) and \(B \in D(Y)\).
The correspondence \(X \times Y \xleftarrow{(\id_X, f)} X \xrightarrow{f} Y\) can be seen as a composition of correspondences in two different ways: For any \(A \in D(X)\) and \(B \in D(Y)\), the first composition maps \((A, B) \mapsto (f_! A, B) \mapsto f_! A \otimes B\), while the latter maps \((A, B) \mapsto A \otimes f^* B \mapsto f_!(A \otimes f^* B)\).
