The algebraic group \(\mathbb{U}_n\) of upper triangular \(n \times n\) matrices with ones on the diagonal, \[ \mathbb{U}_n = \left\{ \begin{pmatrix} 1 & * & * & \cdots & * \\ 0 & 1 & * & \cdots & * \\ \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & \cdots & 1 \end{pmatrix} \right\} , \] is unipotent, as well as any subgroup of \(\mathbb{U}_n\).
