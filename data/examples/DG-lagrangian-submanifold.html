For any smooth manifold \(Q\), consider the cotangent bundle \(T^*Q\) with the canonical symplectic form \(\omega\).
  • Then the zero section of \(T^*Q \to Q\) is a Lagrangian submanifold.
  • The fibers of \(T^*Q \to Q\) are Lagrangian submanifolds.
  • Given a one-form \(\alpha \in \Omega^1(Q)\), the graph \(\Gamma_\alpha \subset T^*L\) is a Lagrangian submanifold if and only if \(\alpha\) is closed.
