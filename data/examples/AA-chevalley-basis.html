Consider the Lie algebra \(\mathfrak{sl}_2\) with Cartan subalgebra \(\mathfrak{h} = \left\{ \left( \begin{smallmatrix} x & 0 \\ 0 & -x \end{smallmatrix} \right) \right\}\) and root system \((\mathfrak{h}^*, \{ \pm \alpha \})\), where \(\alpha\) is given by \(\alpha \left( \left( \begin{smallmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 \end{smallmatrix} \right) \right) = 2\). Then a Chevalley basis for \(\mathfrak{sl}_2\) is given by \[ H_\alpha = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 \end{pmatrix}, \quad X_\alpha = \begin{pmatrix} 0 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 \end{pmatrix}, \quad X_{-\alpha} = \begin{pmatrix} 0 & 0 \\ 1 & 0 \end{pmatrix} . \]
