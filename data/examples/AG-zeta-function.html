For \(X = \PP^m\), we have \(|X(\FF_{q^n})| = \sum_{j = 0}^{m} q^{jn}\) for all \(n\), so \[ Z(\PP^m, t) = \exp \left( \sum_{n = 1}^{\infty} \sum_{j = 0}^{m} \frac{q^{jn} t^n}{n} \right) = \prod_{j = 0}^{m} \frac{1}{(1 - q^j t)} . \] In particular, \[ Z(\star, t) = \frac{1}{1 - t} \quad \textup{ and } \quad Z(\PP^1, t) = \frac{1}{(1 - t)(1 - qt)} . \]
