Let \(A\) and \(B\) be two finite-dimensional algebras over \(k\), and write \(A\textup{-}\textbf{Mod}_\textup{fd}\) for the finite abelian category of \(A\)-modules which are finite-dimensional as vector spaces over \(k\). Then \[ A\textup{-}\textbf{Mod}_\textup{fd} \boxtimes A\textup{-}\textbf{Mod}_\textup{fd} \simeq (A \otimes_k B)\textup{-}\textbf{Mod}_\textup{fd} . \]
