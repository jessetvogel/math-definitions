• A monoid object in the category of sets \((\textbf{Set}, \times, \{ \star \})\) is a monoid.
  • A monoid object in the category of abelian groups \((\textbf{Ab}, \otimes_\ZZ, \ZZ)\) is a ring.
  • For a commutative ring \(R\), a monoid object in the category of \(R\)-modules \((R\textup{-}\textbf{Mod}, \otimes_R, R)\) is an \(R\)-algebra.
  • For any category \(\mathcal{C}\), a monoid in the category of endofunctors of \(\mathcal{C}\), \((\Hom_\textbf{Cat}(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{C}), \circ, \id_\mathcal{C})\), is a monad on \(\mathcal{C}\).
