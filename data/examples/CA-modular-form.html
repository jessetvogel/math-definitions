For \(k \ge 4\) even, consider the Eisenstein series \[ E_k(z) = \sum_{(0, 0) \ne (a, b) \in \ZZ^2} \frac{1}{(a + bz)^k} . \] The conditions on \(k\) are to ensure the series converges. One easily verifies that \[ E_k(z + 1) = E_k(z) \quad \textup{ and } \quad E_k(- z^{-1}) = z^k E_k(z) . \] So, since \(\textup{SL}_2(\ZZ)\) is generated by \(S = \begin{pmatrix} 0 & -1 \\ 1 & 0 \end{pmatrix}\) and \(T = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 \end{pmatrix}\), we conclude that \(E_k\) is a modular form (of level \(\textup{SL}_2(\ZZ)\)) of weight \(k\).
