The convolution of two functions \(f_1, f_2 \in C(G)\) is defined by \[ (f_1 * f_2)(g) = \sum_{xy = g} f_1(x) f_2(y) , \] and pointwise multiplication of \(F_1, F_2 \in C(\widehat{G})\) by \[ (F_1 \cdot F_2)(\chi) = F_1(\chi) F_2(\chi) . \] It can be verified that \[ \mathcal{F}(f_1 * f_2) = \mathcal{F}(f_1) \cdot \mathcal{F}(f_2) \quad \textup{ and } \quad \widehat{\mathcal{F}}(F_1 \cdot F_2) \cdot |G| = \widehat{\mathcal{F}}(F_1) * \widehat{\mathcal{F}}(F_2) . \]
