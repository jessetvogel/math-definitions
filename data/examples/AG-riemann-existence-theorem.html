The Riemann existence theorem relates the étale fundamental group of \(X\) with the fundamental group of \(X(\CC)\). Namely, for any \(x \in X(\CC)\), \[ \pi^\textup{ét}_1(X, x) = \varprojlim_{i \in I} \operatorname{Aut}_X(X_i) \cong \varprojlim_{i \in I} \operatorname{Aut}_{X(\CC)}(X_i(\CC)) = \widehat{\pi_1(X(\CC), x)} . \]
