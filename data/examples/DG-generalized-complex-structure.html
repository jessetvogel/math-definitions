A generalized complex structure generalizes both (almost) complex structures and symplectic structures. Namely, given an almost complex structure \(J : TM \to TM\) and symplectic structure \(\omega : TM \to T^*M\), the endomorphisms \[ \mathcal{J}_J = \begin{pmatrix} -J & 0 \\ 0 & J^* \end{pmatrix} \qquad \textup{and} \qquad \mathcal{J}_\omega = \begin{pmatrix} 0 & -\omega^{-1} \\ \omega & 0 \end{pmatrix} \] are both generalized complex structures.
