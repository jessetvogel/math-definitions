• If \(G = \langle x \;|\; x^m = 1 \rangle\) and \(H = \langle y \;|\; y^n = 1 \rangle\), then \(G * H = \langle x, y \;|\; x^m = y^n = 1 \rangle\).
  • If \(F_n\) is the free group on \(n\) generators, then \(F_m * F_n \simeq F_{m + n}\).
  • The modular group \(\textup{PSL}_2(\ZZ)\) is isomorphic to the free product \((\ZZ/2\ZZ) * (\ZZ/3\ZZ)\).
