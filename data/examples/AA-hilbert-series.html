Let \(R = k[x_1, \ldots, x_n]\) and \(I = (f)\) for some homogenous polynomial of degree \(d\). Then we have an exact sequence (a free resolution) \[ 0 \to R(-d) \xrightarrow{\cdot f} R \to R / I \to 0 , \] which implies that \[ \text{HS}_{R/I}(t) = \text{H}_R(t) - \text{HS}_{R(-d)}(t) = \text{HS}_R(t) (1 - t^d) = \frac{1 - t^d}{(1 - t)^n}. \]
