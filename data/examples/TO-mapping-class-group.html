• The mapping class group of the \(2\)-sphere is \(\textup{MCG}(S^2) \cong \ZZ/2\ZZ\), corresponding to maps of degree \(\pm 1\).
  • The mapping class group of the \(n\)-torus is \(\textup{MCG}(T^n) = \textup{GL}_n(\ZZ)\).
  • The mapping class group of the Klein bottle is \(\textup{MCG}(K) = \ZZ/2\ZZ \times \ZZ/2\ZZ\) is the Klein four-group.
  • The mapping class group of the real projective plane is \(\textup{MCG}(\RR \PP^2) = 1\).
