For any \(n \ge 1\), the symmetric group \(S_n\) is a Coxeter group with \(S = \{ s_1, \ldots, s_{n - 1} \}\), where \(s_i\) exchanges \(i\) and \(i + 1\), and the relations are given by \[ \begin{aligned} s_i^2 &= 1, && \textup{ for } 1 \le i \le n - 1, \\ s_i s_j &= s_j s_i, && \textup{ for } |i - j| \ge 2 , \\ (s_i s_{i + 1})^3 &= 1, && \textup{ for } 1 \le i \le n - 2 . \end{aligned} \]
