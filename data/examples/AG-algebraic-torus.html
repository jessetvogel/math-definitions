Over \(k = \RR\), there are up to isomorphism two algebraic tori of rank one:
  • the torus \(\RR^* = \Spec \RR[t, t^{-1}]\),
  • the unitary group \(U(1) = \Spec \RR[x, y] / (x^2 + y^2 - 1)\). Indeed we have \[ U(1) \times_\RR \CC = \Spec \CC[x, y] / (x^2 + y^2 - 1) \simeq \Spec[z, z^{-1}] \] via the isomorphism \(z = x + iy\) and \(z^{-1} = x - iy\).
