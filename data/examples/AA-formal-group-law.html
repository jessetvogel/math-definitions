• The additive group law is given by \(F(x, y) = x + y\).
  • The multiplicative group law is given by \(F(x, y) = x + y + xy\).
  • The group law \(F(x, y) = (x + y) / (1 + xy)\) appears in special relativity as the addition law for velocities (here the speed of light is set to \(c = 1\)).
