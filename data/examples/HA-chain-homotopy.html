Homotopic maps \(f, g : A^\bdot \to B^\bdot\) yield the same map on the level of homology. Namely, if \(f - g = dk + kd\), then for any \(x \in H^i(A^\bdot)\) we have \[ f(x) - g(x) = d_B^{i - 1}(k^i(x)) + k^{i + 1}(d_A^i(x)) = 0 , \] since \(d_B^{i - 1}(k^i(x)) = 0 \in H^{i}(B^\bdot)\) and \(d_A^i(x) = 0 \in H^{i + 1}(A^\bdot)\), since \(x \in \ker d_A^i\).
