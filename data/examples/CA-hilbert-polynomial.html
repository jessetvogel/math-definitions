Let \(R = k[x_1, \ldots, x_n]\), then \[ \dim R_i = \binom{n - 1 + i}{i} = \frac{(n - 1 + i) \cdots (i + 1)}{(n - 1)!} , \] which is a polynomial in \(i\) for every value of \(n\). In particular, \[ \begin{aligned} \text{HP}_{k[x]}(i) &= 1, \\ \text{HP}_{k[x, y]}(i) &= i + 1, \\ \text{HP}_{k[x, y, z]}(i) &= \tfrac{1}{2} (i + 1)(i + 2), \\ \text{HP}_{k[x, y, z, w]}(i) &= \tfrac{1}{6} (i + 1)(i + 2)(i + 3) . \end{aligned} \]
