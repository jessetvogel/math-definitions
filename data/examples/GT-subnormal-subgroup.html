• Every normal subgroup \(N \subset G\) is subnormal by a chain of length \(1\).
  • From the chain of subgroups \(\{ 1, (1, 2) (3, 4) \} \subset \{ 1, (1, 2) (3, 4), (1, 3) (2, 4), (1, 4) (2, 3) \} \subset S_4\) in the symmetric group \(S_4\) follows that \(\{ 1, (1, 2)(3, 4) \}\) is subnormal in \(S_4\), even though it is not normal in \(S_4\).
