For \(n = 0\) and \(m = 1\), we have that the product of \(f : A \to B\) in \(\text{Ext}^0(A, B) = \Hom(A, B)\) and \(\eta : 0 \to C \to F_0 \to B \to 0\) in \(\text{Ext}^1(B, C)\) is the pullback \[ 0 \to C \to F_0 \oplus_B A \to A \to 0 . \] Similarly, for \(n = 1\) and \(m = 0\), the product of \(0 \to B \to E_0 \to A \to 0\) in \(\text{Ext}^1(A, B)\) and \(g : B \to C\) in \(\text{Ext}^0(B, C) = \Hom(B, C)\) is the pushout \[ 0 \to C \to C \oplus_B E_0 \to A \to 0 . \] It is not hard to verify these sequences are indeed exact.
