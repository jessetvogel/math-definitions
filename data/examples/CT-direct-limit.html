When \(\mathcal{C}\) is the category of sets, groups, rings or modules, a direct limit can explicitly be described by \[ \varinjlim_{i \in I} X_i = \bigsqcup_{i \in I} X_i / \sim{} , \] where \(x_i \in X_i\) is equivalent to \(x_j \in X_j\) if and only if there exists some \(k \in I\) with \(i, j \le k\) such that \(f_{ik}(x_i) = f_{jk}(x_j)\). That is, \(x_i\) is equivalent to \(x_j\) if they ‘eventually become equal’.
