The cusp \(C = \Spec k[x, y] / (x^3 - y^2)\) is not normal since \(k[x, y] / (x^3 - y^2)\) is not integrally closed: the element \(t = y/x\) is integral, as \(t^2 = x\), but does not lie in \(k[x, y] / (x^3 - y^2)\).
Adding this element gives \[ \begin{aligned} k[x, y, y/x] / (x^3 - y^2) &\simeq k[t] \\ x &\mapsto t^2 \\ y &\mapsto t^3 \end{aligned} \] which is integrally closed. This is the normalization of \(C\) \[ \AA_k^1 \to C, \quad t \mapsto (t^2, t^3) . \]
