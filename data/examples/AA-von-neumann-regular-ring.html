• Every field and every division ring is von Neumann regular.
  • For any \(k\) be a field, the matrix rings \(R = \textup{Mat}_n(k)\) are von Neumann regular. Namely, any \(A \in \textup{Mat}_n(k)\) of rank \(r\) can be written as \(A = U \begin{pmatrix} I_r & 0 \\ 0 & 0 \end{pmatrix} V\) using Gaussian elimination, with \(U\) and \(V\) invertible. Now, \(A = AXA\) for \(X = V^{-1} U^{-1}\).
