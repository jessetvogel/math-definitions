The sphere \(S^n \cong \textup{SO}(n + 1) / \textup{SO}(n)\) has a spin structure with \(P = \textup{Spin}(n + 1)\), where the double covering map \(\textup{Spin}(n + 1) \to \textup{SO}(n + 1)\) induces \[ \psi : \textup{Spin}(n + 1) \times_{\textup{Spin}(n)} \RR^n \to \textup{SO}(n + 1) \times_{\textup{SO}(n)} \RR^n \cong T S^n . \]
