A topological manifold can have multiple different smooth structures. Namely, consider the (open) disk \(M = \{ x \in \RR^n : |x| < 1 \}\) as a topological manifold, and define a family of homeomorphisms \[ \varphi_k : M \to M, \quad \varphi_k(x) = |x|^{k - 1} x . \] Then for every \(k \ge 1\), the chart \((M, \varphi_k)\) defines a smooth structure on \(M\). However, these charts are not smoothly compatible since \[ \varphi_\ell \circ \varphi_k^{-1} (x) = |x|^{\ell/k - 1} x \] is not smooth at the origin for \(\ell < k\).
