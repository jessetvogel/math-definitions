• Every abelian group \(G\) is nilpotent. Namely, the center \(Z(G)\) equals \(G\) and we have a series \(G \supset \{ 1 \}\).
  • The quaternion group \(Q_8 = \langle -1, i, j, k \;|\; (-1)^2 = 1 \textup{ and } i^2 = j^2 = k^2 = ijk = -1 \rangle\) is nilpotent. Namely, its center is \(Z(Q_8) = \{ -1, 1 \}\) and we have a series \(Q_8 \supset \{ -1, 1 \} \supset \{ 1 \}\).
