The complete flag variety (corresponding to signature \((1, \ldots, n)\)) is \(\textup{GL}_n(k) / B_n(k)\). Namely, all flags are conjugate via base change, and the stabilizer of any given flag is isomorphic to \(B_n(k)\), the group of invertible upper triangular matrices.
When \(m = 2\), the flag variety parametrizes linear subspaces of \(V\). That is, the flag variety is isomorphic to the Grassmannian \(\textup{Gr}(d_1, V)\).
