There are \(7\) partitions of \(5\):
  • \(5 = 1 + 1 + 1 + 1 + 1\),
  • \(5 = 2 + 1 + 1 + 1\),
  • \(5 = 2 + 2 + 1\),
  • \(5 = 3 + 1 + 1\),
  • \(5 = 3 + 2\),
  • \(5 = 4 + 1\),
  • \(5 = 5\).
The numbers \(p_n\) of partitions of \(n\) can be collected in a generating function \[ P(t) = \sum_{n \ge 0} p_n t^n = \prod_{i = 1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{1 - t^i} \in \ZZ \llbracket t \rrbracket . \]
