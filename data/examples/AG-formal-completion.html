Let \(k\) be a field and consider \(X = \Spec k[x]\) with closed subscheme \(Y \subset X\) given by the ideal \((x)\). Then the formal completion of \(X\) along \(Y\) has a topological space consisting of one point, with sheaf of rings given by \(k \llbracket x \rrbracket\).
Note that this locally ringed space is not the same as \(\Spec k \llbracket x \rrbracket\), whose topological space has two points, given by the prime ideals \((0)\) and \((x)\).
