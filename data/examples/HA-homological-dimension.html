Knowing the global homological dimension \(n\) of a category \(\mathcal{A}\) is useful, as it follows that for any right exact functor \(F : \mathcal{A} \to \mathcal{B}\) the left derived functors \(L^i F A\) are zero for all \(A\) in \(\mathcal{A}\) and \(i > n\).
In particular, for \(F = \Hom_\mathcal{A}(-, B)\) we find \(\textup{Ext}_\mathcal{A}^i(A, B) = 0\) for all \(A, B\) in \(\mathcal{A}\) and \(i > n\).
