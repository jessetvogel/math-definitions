The symmetric algebra of the free \(R\)-module \(M = R^{\oplus n}\) is the polynomial ring \(R[x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_n]\).
The symmetric algebra defines a functor \(\operatorname{Sym} : \textbf{Mod}_R \to \textbf{Alg}_R\) which is left adjoint to the forgetful functor \(\textbf{Alg}_R \to \textbf{Mod}_R\) which sends any \(R\)-algebra \(A\) to its underlying \(R\)-module.
