Let \(n = 2\) and consider the seed \((B, F) = \left(\begin{pmatrix} 0 & 1 \\ -1 & 0 \end{pmatrix}, (x_1, x_2)\right)\). Iterative mutations yield \[ \begin{aligned} (B, F) &\overset{\mu_1}{\mapsto} \left(B^T, \left(\frac{x_2 + 1}{x_1}, x_2\right)\right) \\ &\overset{\mu_2}{\mapsto} \left(B, \left(\frac{x_2 + 1}{x_1}, \frac{x_1 + x_2 + 1}{x_1 x_2}\right)\right) \\ &\overset{\mu_1}{\mapsto} \left(B^T, \left(\frac{x_1 + 1}{x_2}, \frac{x_1 + x_2 + 1}{x_1 x_2}\right)\right) \\ &\overset{\mu_2}{\mapsto} \left(B, \left(\frac{x_1 + 1}{x_2}, x_1\right)\right) \\ &\overset{\mu_1}{\mapsto} \left(B^T, \left(x_2, x_1\right)\right) , \end{aligned} \] where \(B^T\) is the transpose of \(B\). The corresponding cluster algebra is therefore \[ A(B, F) = \QQ\left[ x_1, x_2, \frac{x_2 + 1}{x_1}, \frac{x_1 + 1}{x_2}, \frac{x_1 + x_2 + 1}{x_1 x_2} \right] . \]
