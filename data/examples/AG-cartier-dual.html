• For any finite commutative group scheme \(G\) over \(k\), the Cartier dual of the Cartier dual of \(G\) is isomorphic to \(G\).
  • The Cartier dual of \(\ZZ/n\ZZ\) is \(\mu_n\).
  • Let \(k\) be a field of characteristic \(p\), and let \(\alpha_p = \Spec k[t] / (t^p)\) as a subgroup of the additive group. Then the Cartier dual of \(\alpha_p\) is itself.
