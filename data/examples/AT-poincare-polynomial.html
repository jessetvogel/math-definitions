• \(P(\text{contractible space}) = 1\),
  • \(P(S^n) = 1 + t^n\), where \(S^n\) is the \(n\)-sphere,
  • \(P(\mathbb{T}^n) = (1 + t)^n\), where \(\mathbb{T}^n\) is an \(n\)-torus,
  • \(P(\Sigma_g) = 1 + 2gt + t^2\), where \(\Sigma_g\) is a compact surface of genus \(g\),
  • \(P(\RR \PP^{2n}) = 1\) and \(P(\RR \PP^{2n + 1}) = 1 + t^n\) and \(P(\CC \PP^n) = 1 + t^2 + \cdots + t^{2n}\),
  • \(P(X \sqcup Y) = P(X) + P(Y)\),
  • \(P(X \times Y) = P(X) P(Y)\),
  • \(P(X \vee Y) = P(X) + P(Y) - 1\).
