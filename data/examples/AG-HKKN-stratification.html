Consider \(G = \GG_m^2\) acting naturally on \(X = \AA^2_k\), and let \(\lambda : \GG_m \to G\) be the one-parameter subgroup given by \(\lambda(t) = (t, t^{-1})\). One can only choose \(Z_\lambda^0\) to be equal to the fixed-point locus \(X^\lambda = \{ (0, 0) \}\), which yields \(Z_\lambda = S_\lambda = \{ (x, 0) \in \AA^2_k \}\). Note that \(P_\lambda = G\) and indeed the morphism \(G \times_{P_\lambda} Z_\lambda \to S_\lambda\) is an isomorphism. Therefore, we have constructed an HKKN stratification with \(n = 1\) and \(X_0 = X\) and \(X_1 = X \setminus S_\lambda\).
