The morphism \(\Spec k[x, y, t] / (xy - t) \to \Spec k[t]\) is flat. This follows from \(k[x, y, t] / (xy - t)\) being flat over \(k[t]\), because it is a free \(k[t]\)-module: \[ k[x, y, t] / (xy - t) \simeq k[t] \oplus \bigoplus_{i \ge 1} k[t] \cdot x^i \oplus \bigoplus_{i \ge 1} k[t] \cdot y^i \] However, the morphism \[ \Spec k[x, y, t] / (txy - t) \to \Spec k[t] \] is not flat. Namely, at the maximal ideal \((x, y, t)\), we have that \((k[x, y, t] / (txy - t))_{(x, y, t)}\) is not flat over \(k[t]_{(t)}\), as tensoring the injective map \(k[t]_{(t)} \xrightarrow{\cdot t} k[t]_{(t)}\) does not give an injective map: \(t\) is a zero-divisor in \(k[x, y, t] / (txy - t)\).
