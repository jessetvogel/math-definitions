Let \(A\) be a \(\ZZ\)-module and \(m > 0\) an integer. From the free resolution of \(\ZZ/m\ZZ\), \[ 0 \to \ZZ \xrightarrow{\cdot m} \ZZ \to \ZZ / m \ZZ \to 0 , \] follows that \[ \textup{Tor}_n^\ZZ(\ZZ/m\ZZ, A) = \left\{ \begin{array}{cl} A/mA & \textup{ for } n = 0 , \\ \{ a \in A : ma = 0 \} & \textup{ for } n = 1 . \end{array} \right. \]
