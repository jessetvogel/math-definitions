Consider the following commutative diagram. The snake lemma shows that both rows are exact. Since the kernel and cokernel of the first vertical map are \(H^n(A^\bdot)\) and \(H^{n + 1}(A^\bdot)\), respectively, and similarly for the other vertical maps, applying the snake lemma again yields an exact sequence \[ H^n(A^\bdot) \to H^n(B^\bdot) \to H^n(C^\bdot) \xrightarrow{\delta} H^{n + 1}(A^\bdot) \to H^{n + 1}(B^\bdot) \to H^{n + 1}(C^\bdot) . \] The long exact sequence is now obtained by pasting these exact sequences together.
