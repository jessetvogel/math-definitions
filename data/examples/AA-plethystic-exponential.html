For any \(n \ge 0\), \[ \operatorname{PExp}(t^n) = \exp \left( \sum_{k = 1}^{\infty} \frac{t^{nk}}{k} \right) = \exp \left( - \log(1 - t^n) \right) = \frac{1}{1 - t^n} . \] Furthermore, the plethystic exponential satisfies the following properties:
  • \(\operatorname{PExp}(0) = 1\),
  • \(\operatorname{PExp}(f + g) = \operatorname{PExp}(f) \operatorname{PExp}(g)\) for all \(f, g \in t R \llbracket t \rrbracket\),
  • \(\operatorname{PExp}(-f) = \operatorname{PExp}(f)^{-1}\) for all \(f \in t R \llbracket t \rrbracket\).
