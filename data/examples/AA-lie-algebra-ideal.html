For any Lie algebra \(\mathfrak{g}\), the commutator ideal \([\mathfrak{g}, \mathfrak{g}]\) is an ideal of \(\mathfrak{g}\), given as the set of all elements in \(\mathfrak{g}\) of the form \[ c_1 [X_1, Y_1] + \cdots + c_m [X_m, Y_m] , \] for some constants \(c_i\) and vectors \(X_i, Y_i \in \mathfrak{g}\).
