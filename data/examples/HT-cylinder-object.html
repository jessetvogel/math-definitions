In the category of topological spaces with the natural model structure, a cylinder object of a topological space \(X\) is the product \(X \times [0, 1]\) with the factorization \[ X \coprod X = X \times \{ 0, 1 \} \xrightarrow{i} X \times [0, 1] \xrightarrow{\pi_X} X , \] where \(i\) is the inclusion, and \(\pi_X(x, t) = x\). Indeed \(\pi_X\) is a weak homotopy equivalence.
