The category \(\textbf{Ab}\) of abelian groups is large: the objects (even up to isomorphism) form a proper class. However, it is determined by the smaller category of \(\textbf{Ab}_\textup{fg}\) of finitely generated abelian groups, that is, \(\textbf{Ab}\) is the Ind-completion of \(\textbf{Ab}_\textup{fg}\). This is another way of saying that \(\textbf{Ab}\) is locally presentable.
