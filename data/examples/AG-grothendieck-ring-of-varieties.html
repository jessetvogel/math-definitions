Consider the variety \(\textup{SL}_2 = \Spec k[a, b, c, d] / (ad - bc - 1)\) over a field \(k\). Its class in \(\textup{K}(\textbf{Var}/k)\) is given by \[ \begin{array}{ccccc} [\textup{SL}_2] & = & [\{ (a, b, c, d) \in \AA^4_k \;|\; a = 0, \; bc = -1 \}] & + & [\{ (a, b, c, d) \in \AA^4_k \;|\; a \ne 0, \; d = (1 + bc) / a \}] \\ & = & \mathbb{L} (\mathbb{L} - 1) & + & \mathbb{L}^2 (\mathbb{L} - 1) \\ & = & \mathbb{L}^3 - \mathbb{L} , & \end{array} \] where \(\mathbb{L} = [\AA^1_k]\).
