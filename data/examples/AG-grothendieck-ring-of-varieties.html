• Denote by \(\mathbb{L}\) the class of the affine line \(\AA^1_k\). Then \([\AA^n_k] = \mathbb{L}^n\).
  • From the relation \(\PP^n_k \setminus \PP^{n - 1}_k \cong \AA^n_k\) one shows inductively that \([\PP^n_k] = \mathbb{L}^n + \cdots + \mathbb{L} + 1\) for all \(n > 0\).
  • Consider the variety \(\textup{SL}_2 = \Spec k[a, b, c, d] / (ad - bc - 1)\). Then \[ \def\arraystretch{1.5} \begin{array}{ccccc} [\textup{SL}_2] & = & [\{ (a, b, c, d) \in \AA^4_k \mid a = 0, \; bc = -1 \}] & + & [\{ (a, b, c, d) \in \AA^4_k \mid a \ne 0, \; d = (1 + bc) / a \}] \\ & = & \mathbb{L} (\mathbb{L} - 1) & + & \mathbb{L}^2 (\mathbb{L} - 1) \\ & = & \mathbb{L}^3 - \mathbb{L} . & \end{array} \]
