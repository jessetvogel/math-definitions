First note that \[ \sum_{g \in G} |X^g| = |\{ (g, x) \in G \times X \mid g \cdot x = x \}| = \sum_{x \in X} |G_x| , \] where \(G_x\) denotes the stabilizer of \(x\). From the oribt-stabilizer theorem follows that \[ \sum_{x \in X} |G_x| = \sum_{x \in X} \frac{|G|}{|G x|} = |G| \sum_{O \in X/G} \sum_{x \in O} \frac{1}{|O|} = |X/G| , \] so the result follows.
