Let \(G = D_{n}\) be the dihedral group, and let \(N = \langle \rho \rangle\) be the normal subgroup of rotations and \(H = \langle \sigma \rangle\) the subgroup generated by any fixed reflection \(\sigma\). Then \(H\) acts on \(N\) by conjugation (in particular \(\sigma \rho^k \sigma^{-1} = \rho^{-k}\)), and \(G = N \rtimes H\).
