Let \(X \subset \AA^n_\CC\) be an affine variety defined by polynomials \(f_1, \ldots, f_r \in \CC[x_1, \ldots, x_n]\). Writing \(f_i = \sum_{j, k} a_{ijk} x_j^k\), we see that a spreading-out \(\tilde{X}\) can be defined over \(R = \ZZ[a_{ijk}]\) by the same polynomials \(f_i\), from which it is clear that \(X \simeq \tilde{X} \times_R \CC\).
