Let \(U\) be the complement of \(A\) in \(X\), and let \(\mathcal{F}\) be a sheaf on \(X\). Consider the exact sequence \[ 0 \to \Gamma_A(X, \mathcal{F}) \to \Gamma(X, \mathcal{F}) \to \Gamma(U, \mathcal{F}) , \] and note restriction from \(U\) to \(X\) is surjective when \(\mathcal{F}\) is flasque. Thus an injective resolution \(\mathcal{F} \to \mathcal{I}^\bdot\) gives rise to a short exact sequence \[ 0 \to \Gamma_A(X, \mathcal{I}^\bdot) \to \Gamma(X, \mathcal{I}^\bdot) \to \Gamma(U, \mathcal{I}^\bdot) \to 0 , \] which gives rise to a long exact sequence \[ \cdots \to H_A^i(X, \mathcal{F}) \to H^i(X, \mathcal{F}) \to H^i(U, \mathcal{F}) \to H_A^{i + 1}(X, \mathcal{F}) \to \cdots \]
