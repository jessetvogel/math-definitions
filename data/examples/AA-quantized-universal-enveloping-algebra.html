For \(\mathfrak{g} = \mathfrak{sl}_2(\CC)\), the quantized universal enveloping algebra \(U_q(\mathfrak{sl}_2(\CC))\) is generated by \(E, F, K^{\pm 1}\) and subject to the relations \[ KK^{-1} = K^{-1} K = 1, \quad KEK^{-1} = q^2 E, \quad KFK^{-1} = q^{-2} F, \quad EF - FE = \frac{K - K^{-1}}{q - q^{-1}} . \]
The quantized algebra \(U_q(\mathfrak{g})\) naturally has the structure of a Hopf algebra, given by \[ \Delta(K_i) = K_i \otimes K_i, \quad \Delta(E_i) = E_i \otimes 1 + K_i \otimes E_i, \quad \Delta(F_i) = F_i \otimes K_i^{-1} + 1 \otimes F_i , \] \[ \varepsilon(K_i) = 1, \quad \varepsilon(E_i) = 0, \quad \varepsilon(F_i) = 0 , \] \[ S(K_i) = K_i^{-1}, \quad S(E_i) = -K_i^{-1} E_i, \quad S(F_i) = - F_i K_i . \]
