• Any semisimple category is hereditary.
  • Let \(R\) be a hereditary ring. Then the category of (left) \(R\)-modules is hereditary.
  • Let \(Q\) be a quiver and \(k\) a field. The category \(\operatorname{Rep}_k(Q)\) of quiver representations of \(Q\) over \(k\) is hereditary. In other words, the path algebra of \(Q\) is a hereditary ring.
