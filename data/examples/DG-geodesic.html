Suppose that \(\nabla\) is the Levi-Civita connection on \(M\). Then in terms of local coordinates, the differential equation for a geodesic \(\gamma : I \to M\) is given by \[ \frac{d^2 x^k}{d t^2} + {\Gamma^k}_{ij} \frac{dx^i}{dt} \frac{dx^j}{dt} = 0 , \] where \({\Gamma^k}_{ij}\) denote the Christoffel symbols.
