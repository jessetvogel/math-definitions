• Let \(E\) be a vector space, and \(I\) the set of linearly independent subsets of \(E\). Then \((E, I)\) is a matroid.
  • Let \(G\) be a finite graph, and \(E\) its set of edges. Let \(I\) be the set of subsets of edges that are a forest, that is, subsets of edges that do not contain a cycle. Then \((E, I)\) is a matroid.
