When \(F = \id_\mathcal{C}\) and \(G : \textbf{1} \to \mathcal{C}\), given by \(G(\star) = X\), the comma category \(F \downarrow G\) is the same the slice category \(\mathcal{C}/X\).
When \(F = G = \id_\mathcal{C}\), the comma category \(F \downarrow G\) is the same the arrow category \(\mathcal{C}^\rightarrow\).
