The Lie algebra \(\mathfrak{g} = \mathfrak{sl}_2\) has a basis \[ H = \begin{pmatrix} -1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{pmatrix}, \quad X = \begin{pmatrix} 0 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 \end{pmatrix}, \quad Y = \begin{pmatrix} 0 & 0 \\ 1 & 0 \end{pmatrix} , \] satisfying the relations \[ [H, X] = -2X, \quad [H, Y] = -2Y, \quad [X, Y] = -H . \] This shows that the universal enveloping algebra of \(\mathfrak{sl}_2\) is given by \[ U(\mathfrak{sl}_2) = \CC \langle x, y, z \rangle / (zx - xz + 2x, zy - yz + 2y, xy - yz + z) . \]
