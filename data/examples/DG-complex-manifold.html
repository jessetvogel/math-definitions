Any complex manifold is an almost complex manifold, but the converse does not hold. Consider the \(6\)-sphere inside the (imaginary) octonions \[ S^6 = \left\{ \sum_{i = 1}^{7} x_i e_i \in \mathbb{O} : |x| = 1 \right\} , \] and let \(J : TS^6 \to TS^6\) be given at a point \(p \in S^6\) by multiplication by \(p\), where \(T_p S^6 = p^\perp\). Then \(J\) is defines an almost complex structure, but it is not integrable.
