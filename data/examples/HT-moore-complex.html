Let \(A_\bdot\) be a constant simplicial abelian group, that is, \(A_n = A\) and all face and degeneracy maps are \(\id_A\). Then the Moore complex of \(A_\bdot\) is \[ C(A_\bdot) = \left[ \cdots \xrightarrow{\id_A} \underset{(3)}{A} \xrightarrow{0} \underset{(2)}{A} \xrightarrow{\id_A} \underset{(1)}{A} \xrightarrow{0} \underset{(0)}{A} \to 0 \right] \] and the normalized Moore complex is \[ N(A_\bdot) = \left[ \cdots \underset{(3)}{0} \to \underset{(2)}{0} \to \underset{(1)}{0} \to \underset{(0)}{A} \to 0 \right] . \]
