Let \(G\) be a group and \(H \subset G\) a subgroup of finite index, so that \(\ZZ[G]\) is finitely generated and projective as left and right \(\ZZ[H]\)-module. For any representations \(V\) of \(G\) and \(W\) of \(H\), \[ \operatorname{Ext}_{\ZZ[H]}^i(\operatorname{Res}_H^G(V), W) \isom \operatorname{Ext}_{\ZZ[G]}^i(V, \Hom_{\ZZ[H]}(\ZZ[G], W)) \isom \operatorname{Ext}_{\ZZ[G]}^i(V, \operatorname{Ind}_H^G(W)) , \] where we used \(\Hom_{\ZZ[H]}(\ZZ[G], W) \isom \operatorname{Ind}_H^G(W)\). In particular, for \(i = 0\), we obtain Frobenius reciprocity.
Specializing to \(V = \ZZ\), we obtain an isomorphism in group cohomology \[ \begin{aligned} H^i(G, \operatorname{Ind}_H^G(W)) &= \operatorname{Ext}_{\ZZ[G]}^i(\ZZ, \operatorname{Ind}_H^G(W)) \\ &= \operatorname{Ext}_{\ZZ[G]}^i(\ZZ, \Hom_{\ZZ[H]}(\ZZ[G], W)) \\ &= \operatorname{Ext}_{\ZZ[H]}^i(\ZZ, W) \\ &= H^i(H, W) . \end{aligned} \]
