• Any Lie group \(G\) is parallelizable: any basis \(v_1, \ldots, v_n\) for \(T_e G\) gives a bundle isomorphism \[ \Phi : G \times \RR^n \to TG, \quad (g, (x_1, \ldots, x_n)) \mapsto \left(g, (L_g)_* \sum_{i = 1}^{n} x_i v_i \right) , \] where \(L_g : G \to G\) denotes left multiplication \(h \mapsto gh\).
  • For any \(n \ge 1\), the \(2n\)-sphere \(S^{2n}\) is not parallelizable by the hairy ball theorem.
