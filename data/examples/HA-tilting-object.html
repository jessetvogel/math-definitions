If \(E_1, \ldots, E_n\) is a full strong exceptional sequence then \(X = \bigoplus_{i = 1}^{n} E_i\) is a tilting object. Namely, \(X\) generates \(\mathcal{T}\) as \(E_1, \ldots, E_n\) generate \(\mathcal{T}\), and \[ \Hom_\mathcal{T}(X, X[\ell]) = \bigoplus_{i, j = 1}^{n} \Hom_\mathcal{T}(E_i, E_j[\ell]) = 0 \] for \(\ell \ne 0\) by strongness of the sequence. Finally, the global dimension of the category of \(\Hom_\mathcal{T}(X, X)\)-modules is at most \(n\), by the exceptionality of the sequence.
