Any exact sequence \[ \cdots \rightarrow A_{i - 1} \xrightarrow{f_{i - 1}} A_i \xrightarrow{f_i} A_{i + 1} \rightarrow \cdots \] can be split up into short exact sequences: let \(B_i = \ker f_i = \im f_{i + 1}\), then we obtain short exact sequences \[ 0 \to B_i \to A_i \to B_{i + 1} \to 0 \] for each \(i\).
