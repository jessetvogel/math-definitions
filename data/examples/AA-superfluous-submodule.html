• As \(\ZZ\)-modules, \(\ZZ \subset \QQ\) is a superfluous submodule.
  • More generally, a domain \(R\) is a superfluous submodule in its field of fractions.
  • For any commutative ring \(R\) and finitely generated \(R\)-module \(M\), the submodule \(\mathfrak{J}_R M \subset M\) is superfluous, where \(\mathfrak{J}_R\) denotes the Jacobson radical of \(R\).
