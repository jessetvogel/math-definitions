A scheme is a locally ringed space \((X, \mathcal{O}_X)\) in which every point has an open neighborhood \(U\) such that \((U, \mathcal{O}_X|_U)\) is an affine scheme. A morphism of schemes is a morphism of locally ringed spaces.
One calls \(X\) the underlying topological space, and \(\mathcal{O}_X\) its structure sheaf.
