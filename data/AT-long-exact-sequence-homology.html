Let \[ 0 \rightarrow C' \xrightarrow{i} C \xrightarrow{p} \overline{C} \rightarrow 0 \] be a short exact sequence of chain complexes. Then there is an induced long exact sequence \[ \cdots \rightarrow H_n(C') \xrightarrow{i_*} H_n(C) \xrightarrow{p_*} H_n(\overline{C}) \xrightarrow{\delta} H_{n - 1}(C') \rightarrow \cdots , \] where \(\delta\) is called the connecting homomorphism: for any \(\alpha = [ p(c) ] \in H_n(\overline{C})\) with \(c \in C_n\), we have \(\delta(\alpha) = [ d_n(c) ] \in H_{n - 1}(C')\).
